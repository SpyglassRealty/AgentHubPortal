Quick Fix - Move X Button to Top-Left
Send this to Mission Control Replit:
### Fix: Move Close Button to Avoid Overlap with Vimeo Icons

**Issue:** The X (close) button overlaps with Vimeo's built-in favorite/watch later icons in the top-right corner of the video player.

**Solution:** Move the X button to the top-LEFT corner of the modal.

---

## Update VideoPlayerModal Close Button Position

Find the close button in the VideoPlayerModal component and change its position:

**BEFORE:**
```tsx
{/* Close button */}
<button
  onClick={onClose}
  className={`absolute top-3 right-3 sm:top-4 sm:right-4 z-20 ...`}
>
```

**AFTER:**
```tsx
{/* Close button - TOP LEFT to avoid Vimeo's icons */}
<button
  onClick={onClose}
  className={`absolute top-3 left-3 sm:top-4 sm:left-4 z-20 p-2 rounded-full transition-colors
    ${isDark ? 'bg-gray-800/80 hover:bg-gray-700 text-white' : 'bg-white/80 hover:bg-white text-gray-900'}
    backdrop-blur-sm`}
>
  <X className="w-5 h-5 sm:w-6 sm:h-6" />
</button>
```
