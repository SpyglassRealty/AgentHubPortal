# OpenClaw/Clawdbot Nightly Research — March 1, 2026

## 🔥 Big Story: Google Bans OpenClaw Users from Antigravity

The biggest news in the ecosystem right now. Google mass-banned developers using OpenClaw with their Antigravity platform — some lost access to Gmail and other Google services entirely. Official reason: "malicious usage" causing service degradation from third-party tools consuming too many Gemini tokens. The timing is suspect: Peter Steinberger (OpenClaw creator) joined OpenAI literally last week to lead next-gen personal agents. OpenClaw is dropping all Google support going forward. The thread on r/webdev blew up.

- **Source:** [r/webdev thread](https://www.reddit.com/r/webdev/comments/1rh6fxp/google_just_massbanned_openclaw_users_from/)
- **Takeaway:** Self-hosted and multi-provider setups are now essential. Don't tie your dev identity to a single provider.
