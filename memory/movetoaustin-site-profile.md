# MoveToAustin.org - Site Profile

## Overview
- **URL:** https://www.movetoaustin.org
- **Purpose:** Austin relocation guide — drives leads for Spyglass Realty
- **Traffic:** 3-5k visitors/month
- **CMS:** WordPress with Rank Math SEO plugin
- **Target Audience:** People considering moving to Austin, visitors, relocators
- **Content Person:** Trish (deploys articles after Ryan approves)

## Content Style
- Warm, informative, conversational but professional
- Long-form articles (1500-3000+ words)
- Table of contents for longer pieces
- Internal links to community pages, other blog posts, and key pages
- CTA at end: "Speak to a relocation specialist" or download relocation guide
- Uses bullet points, H2/H3 structure, descriptive subheadings
- SEO meta: title tag, meta description, keywords via Rank Math

1. Engaging intro (2-3 paragraphs)
2. Table of Contents
3. H2 sections with H3 subsections
