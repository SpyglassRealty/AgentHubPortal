# Austin Brokerage Adds 121 Agents While Competitors Contract

## Spyglass Realty expands during market cooling with tech-driven recruiting strategy

AUSTIN, Texas — March 10, 2026 — While most real estate brokerages reduce headcount amid market uncertainty, Austin-based Spyglass Realty has added 121 agents in the past 12 months, growing its roster to 179 agents across Texas.

The expansion comes as Austin's housing inventory increased 24.7 percent compared to 2024, with 47.6 percent of listings experiencing price reductions. Industry data shows many brokerages cutting costs and agent counts as transaction volumes decline.

Spyglass Realty attributes its growth to proprietary recruiting technology through its Mission Control platform, which identifies agents likely to change brokerages based on market performance and career trajectory data.

"Most brokerages are playing defense right now, but we're seeing this as the perfect time to recruit top talent," said Ryan Rodenbeck, founder and CEO of Spyglass Realty. "When the market shifts, the best agents want to be with a company that's investing in their future, not cutting back."

The company plans to reach 300 agents and $1 billion in annual sales volume by the end of 2026. Spyglass currently operates in Austin and Houston, with expansion planned throughout Texas.

Industry analysts note that aggressive recruiting during market downturns can position brokerages for stronger performance when conditions improve. The strategy requires significant capital investment in agent support and technology infrastructure.

Spyglass's Mission Control platform provides agents with market intelligence, lead generation, and transaction management tools. The system also powers the company's recruiting efforts by analyzing market data to identify high-performing agents at competing brokerages.

The real estate market has shown mixed signals in recent months, with inventory stabilizing in some Texas markets while national trends remain volatile. Austin's median home price currently sits at $411,000.
