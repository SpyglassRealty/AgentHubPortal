💡 Outreach Best Practices

⏰Best send times: Tuesday-Thursday, 9-11 AM or 2-4 PM. Avoid Mondays (planning) and Fridays (wrapping up).
📊Response rates: Text (45%) > Social DM (30%) > Email (22%) > Voicemail (8%). Always text first for warm leads.
🔄Follow-up cadence: Day 1 → Day 4 → Day 10 → Day 21 (break-up). 80% of recruiting happens after the 3rd touch.
🚫Never lead with split. Lead with value, culture, or a specific pain point. Split conversations come after interest is established.
🎯Personalization wins: Reference a specific listing, award, or social post. Generic messages get ignored.
