## Consumer Relocation Services

The team at Spyglass Realty understands how transitioning to or from your home can be challenging. - Getting the best price for your property

- Preapproval for a mortgage so you can move fast when you find a property

- Researching properties that match your criteria to save you time

- Finding a community that is right for your family locally or in any city in the US

- Acclimating to the area by providing valuable local resources and information

- Selling an existing home in the Austin area or anywhere you own a property

- Connections to affordable moving companies

- Utility hook up and home services (won't list until you have it )

- Respected settlement services to help you with your closing

- Any other resources that you need for your move!

We are connected with the best real estate companies throughout the country who understand the importance of assisting families make a smooth transition. We are happy to make an introduction free of charge.
