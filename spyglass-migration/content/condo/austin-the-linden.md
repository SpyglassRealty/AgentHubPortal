## About The Linden Residences

The Linden Residences is a contemporary 28-story high-rise offering luxury living in the heart of Austin's vibrant Arts District. Designed to integrate with Austin's unique character, The Linden Residences boasts a limited collection of 117 luxury apartments, each promising stunning views and a harmonious balance between modern design and functionality. Residents can expect top-notch amenities including an outdoor dining and grilling area, a club room, a lap pool, a sauna, and both indoor and outdoor fitness centers.

The Linden Residences' location places residents within easy reach of everything Austin has to offer. The University of Texas at Austin, the traditional Central Business District, the new Medical District, and the world-renowned Blanton Museum of Art are all steps away.
