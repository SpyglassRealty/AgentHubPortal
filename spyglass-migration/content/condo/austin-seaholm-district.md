## About Seaholm District

Seaholm District is located to the north of Lady Bird Lake, San Antonio Street to the east, and to the south of Congress Avenue. It encompasses the parts of 2nd Street and Austin's Central Business District. You can conveniently access the neighborhood by Cesar Chavez, 2nd & 3rd Street, San Antonio Street, and Nueces Street.

Seaholm District is named after Downtown Austin's power plant which was closed indefinitely since the '90s yet its facade with its lightning bolt logo became a well-known icon around Austin. During the 1950s the first phase was constructed. It showcased a mid-century, art deco style which makes it iconic up to this day. As plans of redeveloping the plant started, it was agreed upon along with one of the main tenets, that parts of the plant will remain intact to maintain its historical significance.

The tallest building, The Independent, is located within the Seaholm District. The residential skyscraper stands 58 stories tall and brings 370 condo units to downtown Austin. Aside from the Independent, Seaholm District Austin will be home to Under Armour, Google, Athenahealth, and the Central Library.

 What once was an abandoned power plant is today's treasure in Downtown Austin. Seaholm District has completely transformed bringing in residential buildings, office spaces, amenities, activities, stores, and restaurants for everyone to enjoy. Convenience is loved by the residents in the area. Whatever outdoor activity you are looking for, the surrounding areas of Seaholm District are ready to accommodate you.

Best to beat the hot Texas heat is to rent a kayak or a canoe at Lady Bird Lake. Explore and admire the beauty of Downtown from the waters. For running or cycling, not too far, you'll be able to reach the Ann and Roy Butler Hike and Bike Trail. Open Room is a public art installation with a communal dining table that usually brings in people who would like to picnic and walk their dogs in the yard.

Boiler Nine Bar + Grill is a trendy new American eatery located inside the historic Seaholm Power Plant. It was re-purposed into a two-floor restaurant with an intimate ambiance making it perfect for date nights or dinner with your friends. True Food Kitchen is in the same building as Boiler Nine. A restaurant that serves dishes that are focused on healthy meals such as Grass-fed Burger and Kale Salad. For some delicious coffee to fuel up your morning and afternoon, Merit Coffee is one of the local favorites in the area. The California-based grocery chain, Trader Joe's, the ultimate crowd favorite, is located across True Food Kitchen where you can shop and stock fresh and affordable groceries for your home.

One of the main attractions in Seaholm District Austin is The Central Library. Usually filled during the weekends by students and professionals, The Central Library is admired by everyone who visits due to its modern furniture and grand staircase. Reading rooms, workstations, an amphitheater, and a tech petting zoo for kids to enjoy are some of the highlights when you visit. The Central Library overlooks the gorgeous view of Lady Bird Lake which makes it a truly relaxing experience as you read or work.

For more fun and attractions, in less than 10 minutes drive time, you will be able to reach [South Austin](/south-austin.php). Check out the [hotspots at 78704](/all-things-78704.php) such as the loved Zilker Park, [Barton Springs Pool](/blog/barton-springs-pool-review.html), and the [Barton Creek Greenbelt](/blog/ultimate-guide-to-the-barton-creek-greenbelt-hike-and-bike-trail.html) for more hiking, biking, and rock climbing adventures.
