## 360 Condos Walkthrough

[360 Condominiums](/360-condos-austin.php) is located in the heart of downtown Austin near the Google Sail building and within walking distance of the Seaholm Entertainment District. The residential building stands 44 floors tall and was completed in May 2008.

It was once the tallest building in Austin from January 2008 to June 2009 until it was surpassed by [The Austonian](/the-austonian.php). 360 Condos Tower was developed by Billy Holley and Judd Bobilin of Novare Group and Andrews Urban LLC.

Each unit in 360 [Condominiums](/blog/best-luxury-condominiums-in-downtown-austin.html) features spacious [open floor plans](/blog/how-to-choose-the-right-floor-plan-for-my-home.html), floor-to-ceiling windows, breathtaking views of the Austin skyline and the Texas Hill Country, stainless steel appliances, granite countertops, and a balcony or terrace in every home.

## 360 Condominium Amenities

Exceptional facilities and amenities are enjoyed by the residents of 360 Condos which includes a dedicated 24-hour concierge and 24-hour security. One of the favorite amenities within the building is the 9th Floor Terrace which offers expansive views and ample seating perfect for lounging and dining. 

360's pool is located within the Floor Terrace and contains a sunning shelf with in-water tanning chaise lounges. The water is also heated all year round. There is also an outdoor shower, gas fireplace, plasma TV, free Wi-Fi, four large gas grills in separate areas that are also available within the 9th Floor Terrace.

A club room is located on the 9th floor where residents can bring in their guests. They may also reserve the room for private events. It consists of a full kitchen, a plasma TV, satellite radio, a pool table, and free Wi-Fi. Spend your movie nights within the theater room that offers eight leather club chairs, surround sound TV, DirecTV with premium channels, and a Blu-Ray player.

If you need to hold meetings or get work done with your team, adjacent to the theater room is the conference room which you can also reserve for three hours. Condos for sale in 360 Condos Austin are dog-friendly. The Dog Park on the fourth is open 24/7 for your four-legged friends.

The fitness center is located on the 10th floor of 360 Condos. Precor cardio equipment such as treadmills, elliptical cross-trainers, rowing machines, recumbent bikes, spin bikes, an Adaptive Motion Trainer®, and a full set of Precor weight machines are available for the residents and guests to use. Yoga mats, Bosu balls, medicine balls, stability balls are available within the stretching area.

For your household needs, 360 Residential Condos Austin is only a short walk to the massive Whole Foods. Shops also surround the area especially within the 2nd Street District which features the Moody Theater and restaurants you should visit. Enjoy running, cycling, or taking your dog for a walk at the nearby Ann and Roy Butler Hike and Bike Trail. You may also rent a kayak or canoe at Lady Bird Lake.

Residents can visit the nearby [neighborhoods in 78704](/neighborhoods-in-78704.php).
