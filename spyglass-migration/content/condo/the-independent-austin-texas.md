## The Independent Real Estate Search

The Independent is a residential skyscraper located in Austin, Texas with a height of 690 ft (210 m) which surpassed the Austonian 683 ft (208 ft). The building consists of 58 floors and 370 units that can now be purchased.

The Independent Austin was first proposed in 2014, constructed in 2016, and was completed in the Spring of 2019. Its unique stacked structure definitely stands out from Austin's skyscrapers; whereas the building has been nicknamed "the Tetris building" or "the Jenga tower".

Floor plans within The Independent Austin condos come in one- to four-bedrooms with a range size of 750 to 3,000 square feet. Windows are from ceiling to floor to admire the gorgeous views of Lady Bird Lake and the Texas Hill Country.
