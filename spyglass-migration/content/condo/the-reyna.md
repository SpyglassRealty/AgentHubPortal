## ABOUT THE REYNA

The Reyna is a boutique, sixteen-home urban community nestled in the Dawson neighborhood, off South Congress just minutes to downtown Austin. Offering 2 and 3-bedroom homes designed by local award-winning architects, [Davey McEathron Architecture](http://www.daveymarchitecture.com/) and developed and built by [Heartwood Real Estate Group](http://www.heartwoodrealestate.co/). Each home holds its own unique design, with individually designed floor plans and hand-picked finishes.

You'll call the heart of Austin home living at The Reyna, a stone's throw to the many funky bars, restaurants, shops, and cafes of this vibrant neighborhood.
