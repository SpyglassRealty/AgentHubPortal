## About Residences at 6G

Residences at 6G stand prominently at Sixth and Guadalupe, 600 Guadalupe St, Austin, TX 78701, built by Lincoln Co., San, Kairoi Residential, and DivcoWest. It claims the title of the tallest building in Austin, soaring to 865 feet over 66 stories. The building's functional layout impresses with floors two through 12 dedicated to accommodating 1,626 parking spots and 50 electric car charging stations.

Moving upwards, floors 14 through 32 offer 589,000 square feet of modern office space, while floors 34 through 66 house the luxurious Residences at 6G, a 349-unit apartment complex. This architectural marvel was brought to completion in 2023.
