## Spring Condos Austin Walkthrough

Located in the heart of Downtown Austin, [Spring Condos Austin](/spring-condos-austin.php) is a [luxury condominium](/blog/best-luxury-condominiums-in-downtown-austin.html) situated at the intersection of W 3rd St and Bowie St. This 433 ft tall structure consists of 43 floors developed by Zenith Partners Ltd. with exquisite designs created by renowned architects Rafii Architects and Morris Architects.

Towering as the 8th highest skyscraper in Austin, Spring Condos is a modern and innovative point tower - which means it has an upright build above a broad mid-rise base.

Spring Condos is the perfect place to call home, featuring exquisite granite countertops and top-of-the-line Bosch appliances. ## Spring Condos Real Estate Search

Spring Condos Austin is a 433 ft tall luxury condominium located in the heart of Downtown Austin at 300 Bowie Street. It was completed in 2009 making it the second tallest tower in Austin. Today, Spring Condos stands as the first point tower and the 8th tallest tower in the city. The slender built residential condominium stands with 43-floor and was developed by Foad Rafii of Rafii Architects, a Vancouver-based architect. Pentagram was the graphic designer of the project while Morris Architects was the architect of record.

Each unit features granite countertops and high-grade Bosch appliances. Spring Condos offers its residents efficient floor plans and gorgeous views of Lady Bird Lake and Downtown Austin.
