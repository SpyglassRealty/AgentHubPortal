## About 78701 Homes for Sale

78701 is one of the affluent and popular zip codes in Austin that covers downtown Austin. The growing number of developments in the area has turned downtown into a mecca of cool and comfort. With its diverse industry with plenty of unique, stylish, luxurious condos to live in, the 78701 area is the center of lively lifestyles, creativity, and culture. The area appeals to all kinds of homebuyers from young professionals, and families, to individuals looking into [downsizing](/blog/downsizing-your-home-austin-tx.html). The developments in residential condos in the area, it has greatly influenced the urban living scene and has reshaped downtown Austin.

The area of 78701 is home to a plethora of skyscraper condominiums. If you are thinking of investing, relocating, or living within downtown Austin, you'll be able to find a condo that will suit your lifestyle and needs.
The Independent, the tallest residential building in Austin stands 690 ft tall with 58 floors and 370 condo units. Its structure stands out due to its uniqueness wherein it earned its nicknames Tetris Building and the Jenga Tower. The second tallest tower in the area is The Austonian, which stands 683 feet with 56 floors located on Congress Avenue. Before the Austonian, Austin 360 condos were once the tallest in Austin with 44 floors and 460 units. And the newly constructed Airbnb-powered condo, Natiivo Austin stands 33 stories with 249 units featuring 10ft ceilings and handpicked furnishing.
