## About the Great Hills Neighborhood

Great Hills was originally developed during the '70s and up to the early 2000s homes were being built. The neighborhood attracts families due to its quiet and convenient location to major local highways, entertainment venues, shopping centers, and restaurants. The neighborhood is home to scenic views, gorgeous tree-filled terrains, and unique homes that vary in style.
Plenty of amenities within the neighborhood of Great Hills. Residents can hike or bike around the area and enjoy the scenic views of the Texas Hill Country. The Great Hills Park is not too far from the neighborhood. The said park cover 80 acres with a 1.7-mile hiking trail. Aside from that, there is also a playscape, covered pavilion, fitness equipment, and picnic tables within the park.
Nearby the vicinity, the Bull Creek Park is a 48-acre hidden gem in Great Hills. Bring your dog for a walk, enjoy a dip on the natural springs, and waterfalls. A barbecue pit, fishing pier, and volleyball courts are available in the park. For golf enthusiasts, the Great Hills Country Club has complete amenities perfect for swimming, tennis, and a clubhouse for gathering.
Residents who work within the tech area of [North Austin](/north-austin.php) has easy access to the place by driving or commuting. If you are planning to visit [Downtown Austin](/downtown-austin.php), it will take about less than 30 minutes to reach the area. You won't have a problem looking for shopping centers in Great Hills, The Arboretum is a popular shopping center that is close to the neighborhood where residents can enjoy shopping, dining, cinemas, and bookstores.
