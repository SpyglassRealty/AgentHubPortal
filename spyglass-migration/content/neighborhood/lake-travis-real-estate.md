## About the Neighborhood

 Lake Travis is one of Austin's most scenic natural areas. It is the land that borders the Colorado River and stretches above the Mansfield Dam. Residents and visitors around central Texas enjoy all sorts of activities available in the area that are seeking outdoor fun. With 271 miles of shoreline, it is the most active reservoir in Texas.

Located just about 30 minutes away from [downtown Austin](/downtown-austin), the neighborhood offers gorgeous rolling hills and scenic views. Lake Travis homes for sale are ideal for people looking for houses near the waterscapes without being too far from the city. Residents love the exceptional waterfront views of the lake which is perfect for luxury living. Lake Travis homes for sale range from the high-$200s to over two million.

Catch the sunset, go on zipline adventures, rent a boat, go swimming...there are a lot of things you can do in the area with your friends and family. Lake Travis area real estate includes master-planned communities, luxury estates, and small neighborhoods with spectacular Hill County views. These neighborhoods are filled with different amenities for their residents such as golf courses, waterparks, shopping, dining, and entertainment areas. 

Here are a few of the most popular communities in the area:

### [Steiner Ranch](/steiner-ranch-real-estate.php)

A 4,600 master-planned community located in Northwest Austin bordered by Lake Austin and Lake Travis. The community has dedicated 1500 acres to wildlife habitats, parks, and greenbelt. Nature enthusiasts will enjoy the green spaces within proximity to their homes.

### [Hills of Lakeway](/hills-of-lakeway-austin.php)

Hills of Lakeway offers traditional homes in a guard-gated neighborhood close to golf courses and green spaces. Residents love living in the area because of the outdoor living areas for entertainment and convenient access to shopping and dining centers.

### [Rough Hollow](/rough-hollow-lakeway.php)

The 1,800-acre community offers elegant custom homes with enviable amenities. Rough Hollow has 19 neighborhoods in 6 sections. The prices of the homes in Rough Hollow may depend on the view and amenities surrounding the home. Highland Village and Rough Hollow Harbor Yacht Club are some of the great amenities in the area.

### [Lakeside Tessera on Lake Travis](https://spyglassrealty.com/lakeside-tessera-on-lake-travis.php)

Lakeside Tessera on Lake Travis houses for sale is an 875-acre master-planned community located in Lago Vista, Texas. The neighborhood offers an urban oasis of amenities and recreation without being too far from the big city. Residents in the area can enjoy plenty of amenities that are available in the area. Homes for sale in Lakeside Tessera starts in the mid-$200s design-built by the best and talented designers and builders hand-picked by Lakeside Tessera. 

### [Lake Pointe](/lake-pointe-austin.php)

Another gorgeous master-planned community close located in the Texas Hill Country near Lake Austin and Lake Travis and about 12 miles to the west of downtown Austin. This neighborhood is popular with young families, retirees and those who love to live an active lifestyle. Lake Pointe residents have easy access to many amenities such as beaches, pools, parks, and golf courses. There are also shopping areas and restaurants nearby. The average price for homes for sale in Lake Pointe is at around $400s and the average is 3200 sq ft.

### [Rocky Creek](/rocky-creek-austin.php)

## Things to do in Lake Travis, Texas

Lake Travis is the most visited freshwater recreational vacation destination in Texas. Water enthusiasts enjoy the fresh and clear blue waters that are the results of the stunning limestones on the bottom. If you plan on visiting or living in Lake Travis area real estate, here are some of the fun activities you can do in the area.

There are plenty of lakeside parks where you can go take your family on a picnic. swimming, or even hiking. Well-known parks in the area are Windy Point Park where families can enjoy the gorgeous scenery of the lake. Mansfield Dam Park, a haven to nature enthusiasts that offers panoramic views of the lake with a wide array of amenities. Pace Bend Park, located in the west Travis County with more than nine miles of shoreline along the scenic lake.

[Waterparks in Lake Travis](https://laketravis.com/lake-travis-category/waterparks/) are definitely huge in the lake. Enjoy an island adventure at Lake Travis Waterloo Adventures that features a 600-foot water playground with around 20 obstacles. Near the popular lake is Krause Springs, a camping and swimming site known for the 32 springs on the property plus its man-made pool and natural pool that flows to the lake.

Boat rentals are also available. Whether you want to rent a houseboat, wakeboard, sailboat, kayak, paddleboards, or jet ski, there are plenty of watercraft options to choose from. Residents are also close to shopping centers, restaurants, vineyards, golf courses, and breweries. There are also several entertainment venues and bars that offer live music.

If you are coming to check the area from another city or state, finding a place to stay won't be a problem! Nature enthusiasts will enjoy campgrounds, houseboats, or RV parks. While to those looking for a luxurious night, hotels, vacation rentals, bed and breakfasts are also abundant in the area.
