Lakeside at Tessera on Lake Travis is a master-planned community located in Lago Vista, Texas on the North Shore of [Lake Travis](/lake-travis-real-estate.php). The 875-acre community is about an hour away from [Downtown Austin](https://spyglassrealty.com/downtown-austin.php). Tessera on Lake Travis offers an urban oasis of amenities and recreation with the convenience of city living. Developed by the award-winning real estate development, Hines, Tessera on Lake Travis Homes for sale starts at the mid-$200s and design-built by exceptional builders. Lakeside Tessera on Lake Travis was awarded as the best master-planned community by the Austin Business Journal in 2019.

## Lakeside at Tessera on Lake Travis Neighborhood

Lakeside Tessera on Lake Travis is framed by Lake Travis to the south and the vast Balcones Canyonlands Wildfire Refuge to the west and north. The community offers convenient access to Northwest Austin shops, restaurants, and entertainment venues. Aside from that it also has access to the businesses within Round Rock, [Cedar Park](/cedar-park-real-estate.php), and [Leander](/leander-homes-for-sale.php).

The community has amenities that focus on nature. If you are a fan of the great outdoors from hiking, biking, swimming, or wakeboarding, Tessera on Lake Travis is the perfect place for you. These amenities are close to your home as soon as you step out of your home. Some f the amenities within the community are a fishing dock, playground, infinity pool, event pavilion, 1-mile shoreline beach access, exercise lawn, and miles of hike and bike trail.

One of the best amenities is the proximity of the neighborhood to the shoreline of Lake Travis. Residents can enjoy the stunning surroundings and appreciate the lake lifestyle.
