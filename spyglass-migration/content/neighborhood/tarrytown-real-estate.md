## Something About Tarrytown

Tarrytown is known as one of the most expensive and luxurious neighborhoods in the city. Mostly residential, but there are also businesses and restaurants in the neighborhood that is convenient for the residents to access. [Downtown Austin](/downtown-austin.php) and [South Austin](/south-austin.php) can be reached in just a short drive for more shopping options, restaurants, and entertainment venues. Tarrytown located to the west of MoPac and close to the University of Texas - Austin.

Homes in Tarrytown as mostly traditional Southern and modern. It is home to the young and old, professionals, and families. Its charm, neatness, and classiness drive Austinites to live in the neighborhood. Tarrytown is one of the most affluent and desirable areas to live in Austin. Mansions in the area large but there are also some apartments, condos, smaller and older homes as well. There is also plenty of green space nearby, such as its proximity to Lake Austin, as well as parks and swimming pools and trail great for hiking and biking.

When it comes to outdoor recreation, residents can easily access the six-acre Reed Park which has a swimming pool, playing field, barbeque pit, and a nature trail. For golf enthusiasts, the Lions Municipal Gold Course is open and has been visited by golf professionals. Mayfield Preserve is not that far from the neighborhood as well which is a larger park that features beautiful gardens and ponds. Deep Eddy, a man-made spring-fed pool, will save you from the Texas heat during summer; they have movie nights you will enjoy!

Some closeby neighborhoods are Rollingwood, [West Lake Hills](/westlake-real-estate.php), and Old West Austin.
