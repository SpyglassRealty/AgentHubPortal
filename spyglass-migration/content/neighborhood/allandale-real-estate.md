## About the Allandale Neighborhood

Allandale is one of the most ideal neighborhoods to live in because of its accessibility to many local roads, shops, and green spaces. The neighborhood is close to major roads such as MoPac, Rt. 2222, IH-35, 183 and 360 which makes it convenient for the residents to drive or commute. Allandale is one of the walkable neighborhoods in Austin because of its low traffic and quiet feel. The neighborhood attracts couples with small children because of the safety, quality schools, and parks in the area.
There are also plenty of amenities within the neighborhood. such as Lake Austin, Beverly Sheffield Park, Northwest Recreation Center, and Northwest District Park. Residents are close to Downtown's 6th Street where they can enjoy the lively nightlife.
Homes for sale in Allandale come in different styles. Most of the homes were built in the '50s and '60s. Although the area is becoming an urban location, modern, luxury, and custom homes are being built in the area. The average home price for homes in Allandale starts at the mid $300s.
