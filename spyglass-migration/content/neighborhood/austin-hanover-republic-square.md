## About Hanover Republic Square

Hanover Republic Square is a premier high-rise apartment community located in the heart of downtown Austin, Texas. This exceptional property offers a luxurious and sophisticated living experience, with its 45-story tower comprising 310 stunning apartment homes. Situated just steps away from Lady Bird Lake, residents of Hanover Republic Square enjoy unparalleled access to the vibrant and thriving downtown Austin community.

## Hanover Republic Square Amenities

Hanover Republic Square boasts a remarkable range of amenities designed to enhance residents' lifestyles. The community features a state-of-the-art fitness center, perfect for those seeking an active and healthy lifestyle. Residents can also relax and unwind in the stunning rooftop swimming pool, offering breathtaking views of Lady Bird Lake and the Austin skyline.

For entertainment and socializing, there are beautifully landscaped outdoor gathering areas with grilling stations and fire pits. Residents have access to a business center and conference room, making it convenient for remote work or meetings.

## Around the Neighborhood

Hanover Republic Square is nestled in one of Austin's most sought-after neighborhoods, offering residents the best of city living. With its prime location, residents have easy access to a plethora of dining, shopping, and entertainment options. Explore the lively entertainment district on 6th Street or indulge in the vibrant culinary scene at the nearby South Congress Avenue.

Nature enthusiasts will appreciate the proximity to Lady Bird Lake, perfect for outdoor activities such as hiking, biking, and kayaking. The property is also conveniently located near major employers, educational institutions, and transportation hubs, ensuring a seamless urban lifestyle for its residents.
