## About Santa Rita Ranch Austin

Santa Rita Ranch is a 3,100 master-planned community located in Liberty Hill, TX with a part of it located in Georgetown. In order to reach the community, it can be accessed from Santa Rita Blvd and Ronald Reagan Blvd. Residents enjoy the friendly neighborhood as well as the community promoting a sense of belonging for each and everyone residing in the area. Santa Rita Ranch Austin has its own Ranch Code where it encourages the residents to preserve the community's values. Despite being in a suburban area, the community is situated in an area that is abundant in nature. If you plan on visiting [downtown Austin](/downtown-austin.php), it's about 40 minutes away from the community.

Homes for sale in Santa Rita Ranch Austin starts from $200s to over a million while floor plan sizes range from 1,200 square feet to 3,800 square feet. An average single-family home consists of four bedrooms and baths. The median sale price for Santa Rita Ranch Austin home for sale is $330,400.

## Things to Do in Santa Rita Ranch Austin

Residents within the community will love the wide array of available activities in the area. The one of a kind master-planned community offers stunning and resort-like amenities for everyone to enjoy. One of the most favorite places of the homeowners living in Santa Rita Ranch is The Green, an outdoor amenity-filled with plenty of activities for the young and old. Whether you are looking to escape the hot summer heat in the splash zone or relax and admire the gorgeous scenery and fresh air of the Hill Country, The Green awaits for you.

Children will definitely love playing within The Green Play Park which includes a rope play tower and splash zone. Stay strong and fit at the outdoor Fitness Zone which offers state of the art equipment and aesthetics of the Danish design for an easy place in any environment. Play a game of basketball at the nearby Basketball Court which is available for day and night within the area as well.

Hike and bike trails are also available in the area. You don't need to go too far to experience and connect with nature. If you are looking for a quick grub, a food truck lot is available within the area! You'll never run out of things to do at Santa Rita Ranch for there are plenty of amenities and neighborhood activities you can participate in.
