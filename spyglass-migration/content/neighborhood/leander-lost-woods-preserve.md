## Where is Lost Woods Preserve Located?

Lost Woods Preserve, a unique new home [community in Leander, TX](/blog/lost-woods-preserve-community.html), sits at the corner of Journey Parkway and CR 175/Sam Bass Road. The community is located 40 minutes from [Downtown Austin](/downtown-austin.php), 20 minutes from [Round Rock](/round-rock-real-estate.php), and 15 minutes from [Cedar Park](/cedar-park-real-estate.php).

## Lost Woods Preserve Homes

Lost Woods Preserve will feature around 220 single-family homes upon completion. The community will offer 13 distinct floor plans, ranging from 1825 square feet, with prices starting at approximately $600,000. For those looking for a move-in ready option, inventory homes are available for sale, while lots are also available for purchase to build from the ground up. The tax rate for the community is 2.37%, and homeowners association fees are around $65 per month.

## Schools Near Lost Woods Preserve

Aikens Elementary, Styles Middle School, and Ralph High School are located in the Leander ISD area, and the community is renowned for its close proximity to these schools, with the elementary and middle schools being only about a mile away. Ralph High School is approximately four or five miles away. Downtown Austin is 26.8 miles from the community, while the Metro Rail Leander station is only 6.8 miles away. 

## Amenities in Lost Woods Preserve

Lost Woods Preserve offers a variety of amenities to its residents, such as a pool, playground, and volleyball courts. The community is situated right across from the Williamson County Regional Park, which provides access to several sports fields for soccer, softball, and football, as well as tennis and basketball courts, a 400-meter track, bike, and hike trails, a disc golf course, and a splash pad.
