## About Mueller

Mueller is an award-winning master-planned community located in East Austin, three miles from Downtown Austin and two miles away from the University of Texas. Mueller is a sustainable, transit-oriented community that includes a broad range of homes and amenities for its residents. This master-planned community in Austin started in 2007 on the grounds of the old Austin Mueller Airport. It was redeveloped into a mixed-use urban village and is envisioned as a sustainable community that meets its goals in housing and economic development.

## Mueller Real Estate

Mueller Austin's aspects is inspired by the greater Austin community's friendly character. Their 700-acre site is becoming home to 14,300 people, 14,500 employees, and 10,500 construction jobs. Homes in Mueller are resource-efficient, they use non-toxic and recyclable materials to help maintain and improve air and water quality.

It is one of the ideal places for families, empty nesters, professionals, and retirees since the area provides easy access to essential stores, hospitals, and major roads in the surrounding areas. Home for sale in Mueller Austin ranges from $150s to $500s with a variety of home styles such as yard houses, rowhouses, and garden houses. More than 1,400 new houses, condos, and apartments are specifically reserved for households lower than the area's median income which makes Mueller one of the largest communities that offer affordable and workforce housing in Central Texas. Mueller home builders include David Weekley Homes, CalAtlantic Homes, Wes Peoples Homes, and Empire Communities.

## Things to do in Mueller

There are plenty of outdoor opportunities in Mueller. The Mueller Retail Center includes approximately 400,00 sqft of retail shops. This place is a one-stop-shop where you can shop, dine, and run banking errands. The Mueller Market District is located by the borders of 51st St and Berkman Drive. It serves the community with retail shops, eateries, and an 83,000 sqft H-E-B grocery store.
