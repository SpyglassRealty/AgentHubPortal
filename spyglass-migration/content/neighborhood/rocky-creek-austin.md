## About Rocky Creek Austin

Rocky Creek is located to the west of Austin within the [Bee Cave](/bee-cave-real-estate.php) / [Lakeway](/lakeway-homes-for-sale.php) area that features 438 acres of Hill Country Living and 300 acres of dedicated green spaces. Residents will enjoy the luxury of nature and Hill Country view without being too far from the big city. The neighborhood will feature 395 homes once completed. Rocky Creek Neighborhood homes for sale floor plans range from 2,500 to 4,000 square feet. 

Residents of Rocky Creek Austin will enjoy plenty of amenities where kids and adults will love. Rocky Creek features an amenity center that includes a resort pool and playground, and a nature trail for hiking and biking. The Hamilton Pool Preserve is not too far from Rocky Creek where you can relax underneath the stunning limestone structure and take a dip in the pool to cool down during the summer. There are more amenities and water sports in the nearby [Lake Travis](/lake-travis-real-estate.php) as well. Local shops are thriving in the area. The Hill Country Galleria takes about six minutes from the neighborhood where residents can dine and shop.

For those golf enthusiasts, Spanish Oaks has a golf course that is named by Golf Digest as one of the best courses in Texas. Falconhead Golf Club also has designed courses with facilities for beginners and a clubhouse for gatherings.

The distance from Rocky Creek Austin to Downtown takes about less than 30 minutes while the distance to the Bergstrom Internation Airport can take up to 40 minutes. Public transportation is also readily available in the area making it convenient for the residents to get around.
