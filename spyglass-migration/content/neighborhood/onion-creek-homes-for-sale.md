Onion Creek is an established, golf-course community located fifteen minutes to the south of Austin that offers gorgeous estates that are surrounded by mature trees, well-maintained lawns, and the greens of the Onion Creek Club. This peaceful community has its name taken from the creek that starts in Hays County and connected to the Colorado River. Onion Creek is served by the Austin Independent School District.
The community was established in 1974 with the majority of its homes built prior to 1990. Onion Creek offers homes and condos for residents that are looking for a home that will suit their lifestyle. Homes and condos for sale in Onion Creek are from the $250s to the $900s. Luxury homes in the community consist of floor-to-ceiling windows, There are plenty of condos and townhouses in and nearby the community. The Pointe at Onion Creek Condominiums is a gated condo community with 32 free-standing units that were constructed in the mid-1980s. Another community near Onion Creek is Windrock Condominiums that offers 26 units.
Onion Creek is located to the south of Austin within the 78747 zip code. The community can be accessed on I-35 to the south of Slaughter Ln with convenient access to the interstate that will take you to North Austin and Round Rock while Buda, San Marcos, and San Antonio to the south. If you are coming from the Austin-Bergstrom International Airport, it will take you about 25 minutes to Onion Creek. Downtown Austin is just about 10 minutes away from the community where residents can easily access the amenities and lively entertainment districts.

## Things to Do in Onion Creek

The community offers plenty of recreational opportunities for its residents to enjoy. Homes within the community were built around the Onion Creek Club and Golf Course. Amenities in the community include a fitness center, tennis court, a restaurant, a large clubhouse, and a resort-style swimming pool. Pet owners will also love residing in the area due to the community being a pet-friendly place. For groceries, shopping, and dining options, Austin and San Marcos are just a few minutes away.
