## About Headwaters Dripping Springs

Located in Dripping Springs, about 25 minutes from Austin, Headwaters is an award winning 1,035-acre master-planned community that offers distinctive neighborhoods, panoramic views of the Hill Country, and sustainable homes built by the best home builders in the nation.

Homes for sale in Headwaters are inspired by the ranch land's rich heritage and timeless geography with various home styles. The Hill Country Modern which is the new contemporary signature style at Headwaters.  The signature homes feature open floor plans with tall ceilings and windows to showcase the gorgeous outdoors. The Hilltop at Headwaters is the newest neighborhood in the community that stands out and boasts the magnificent views of the community. The Hilltop's limited release of 12 premier homesites provides an opportunity for those who want to build a home on the elevated enclave of the most sought-after lots at Headwaters.

## Things to Do in Headwaters

Residents in Headwaters enjoy a wide array of amenities designed to enhance the community's way of living. The HW Central is the gathering core of Headwaters; a destination all on its own. The HW Complex includes a fitness center, a playfield, and an event pavilion for the year-round programs and activities within the community.

HW Fit is a  state-of-the-art fitness facility in HW Central offering residents access to a group fitness area and innovative cardio and strength equipment. HW Fit has close proximity to the eight miles of trail systems surrounding the community to help expand your workout routine. Relax or get a little work done within the community's coffee shop, The Hub. For more recreational space, the Homestead Park features a croquet lawn, stargazing amphitheaters, a picnic area, and more. While the Headwaters Park is located to the north of the community the offers trails, greenbelt, and play areas.
