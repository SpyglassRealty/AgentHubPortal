## Avery Ranch Neighborhood

If you are looking for a community that offers a sense of belonging, a quick drive to downtown, several amenities, and a stunning scenic view from your home, Avery Ranch homes for sale is one of the best places to settle down. Northwoods and Pearson Place at Avery Ranch are two of the most popular neighborhoods in the area.
Avery Ranch is located in the northwest corner of Austin, Texas and it only takes about a 20-minute drive to reach [downtown Austin](/downtown-austin.php). Residents also have easy access to public transportation with the Austin Cap Metro Lakeline train station being within walking distance.
The neighborhood is rich when it comes to amenities which includes 5 community centers encompassing 17 acres. Amenities in the area offers tennis courts, hiking and biking trails, sand volleyball, basketball courts, and a 25-meter swimming pool. Aside from that, there is also an outdoor amphitheater, lap pool, children's beach pool, tanning pool, and spa. The neighborhood is loaded with plenty of activities that will keep your community spirit busy all year round.
Avery Ranch is also known for its 18-hole, 226 acre Avery Ranch Golf Club which is one of the premier golf courses in Austin. It borders a 60-acre lake with natural features that include springs, ponds, creeks, and vegetation the residents can enjoy. This golf course was rated as one of the best golf courses by Avid Golfer magazine because of its golf conditions, innovative design, dining area, and clubhouse.
For residents that are interested to join the neighborhood's swim team, the Avery Ranch Redfish Swim Team is open for their enjoyment and competing within the North Austin Aquatics League. Avery Ranch Homeowners Association also founded Boy Scout Troop 371 while the Avery Ranch Social Committee designed a program Avery Ranch Gives Back that is about donating items to organizations in need.
