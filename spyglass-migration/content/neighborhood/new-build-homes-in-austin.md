From the amenities, cost of living, housing, and livability, Austin is surely a desirable place to work, play, and live in.

Whether you're moving to Austin or relocating to a new part within the city, there are plenty of neighborhoods to choose from that will suit your lifestyle. [North Austin](/north-austin.php) boasts its panoramic views of the Texas Hill country, quiet and established neighborhoods. For outdoor recreation and an ambiance like no other, [West Austin](/west-austin-homes-for-sale.php) might be for you. For homes with a mix of historic and new construction; [East Austin](/east-austin.php) is an ideal place to live where most millennials are moving within the area. [South Austin](/south-austin.php), the trendiest area in Austin that showcases many local favorites as well as popular attractions and green spaces for recreation.
