Circle D-KC is located 6 miles northeast of Bastrop and provides a unique opportunity to own properties in a peaceful, laid-back community surrounded by nature and Lake Bastrop. Circle D offers plenty of space to relax with homesites that range from one to five acres. This well-established neighborhood has ranch-style, single-family homes situated on spacious lots within the beautiful rural setting. Circle D features various recreational amenities for residents to enjoy the community's natural beauty. Homeowners get the advantages of living in a country setting while having convenient access to major shopping, dining, and event venues nearby.

Circle D homes for sale are located to the northwest of FM 1441 and Texas State Highway 21 in Bastrop in the 78602 zip code. Students residing in Circle D will attend school under Bastrop ISD.

Bastrop is a rural community ideal for residents who want to be close to nature and live in farmland while being able to access the amenities of a big city. Bastrop has plenty of green fields, golf courses, swimming holes, and recreational facilities for families to enjoy. The area offers a big city feel despite being a small town.
