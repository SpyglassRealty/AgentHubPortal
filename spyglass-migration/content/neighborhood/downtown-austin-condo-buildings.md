Downtown Austin is a great place to live especially if you're looking for residences with great accessibility to major businesses, entertainment, and dining. The area is home to many [luxury condos](/blog/best-luxury-condominiums-in-downtown-austin.html) and lofts that offer a variety of amenities including swimming pools, recreation rooms, fitness centers, and much more.

[Downtown Austin](/downtown-austin.php) has seen a lot of development in recent years and is now home to several new high-rise residential buildings including The Austonian, 360 Condominiums, The Independent, and [upcoming developments](/blog/upcoming-downtown-austin-condos.html) such as The Waterline Tower and 321 West. 

Major corporations such as Google and Meta have relocated or expanded their operations to downtown Austin. The area is also home to many tech startups which continue to grow and expand in the city.

## Is Downtown Austin a good place to live?

This urban core of Austin is a gem filled with [restaurants](/blog/best-restaurants-in-austin.html), bars, and iconic [landmarks and murals](https://www.movetoaustin.org/blog/landmarks-attractions-murals-in-austin-map-included/) you won't find anywhere else. Living in downtown Austin has its perks and there's always something going on. 

The nightlife scene is flourishing in downtown Austin, with the hottest clubs and bars lining 6th Street. 6th Street has a mixture of live music venues, restaurants, and bars that make it the perfect place to hang out any day or night of the week.

There are also plenty of opportunities for shopping and entertainment in other areas of downtown Austin. The Warehouse District has some great shops from handcrafted items to apparel owned by locals.

## Where Should I Live in Downtown Austin?

If you're considering moving to a condo in downtown Austin, you'll find all the information needed here. If you're looking for luxury living, there are plenty of high-end condos in downtown Austin that will provide all the amenities you need.

## Can I Get Around Downtown Austin Without a Car?

Downtown Austin is the most [walkable](/blog/best-walkable-neighborhoods-in-austin.html) part of the city, with a great mix of restaurants, bars, and shopping. You can walk to all those things from your condo in downtown Austin. There are also plenty of [public transportation](/blog/austin-public-transportation.html) options that make it easy for you to get around without a car.
