## About Easton Park Homes for Sale

Easton Park Austin has a size of 1,500 acres and has 350+ acres of green space for its residents making with multiple community parks, interconnected hiking and biking trails, resort-style swimming pool, an amenity center, and a club-style fitness facility!  Community events are also held in the area such as a fitness session in Great Lawn at Bryant Park, food truck Fridays, and annual Parkapalooza Extravaganza.
The community is located at 7804 Skytree Dr. Austin, TX, 78744 and developed by Brookfield Residential. They also handle community management, homeowner concerns, lifestyle events, and more.

Easton Park was inspired by some of the popular parks around the world with an Austin edge. The goal is to create a community that is an extension of Austin surrounded by things all Austinites love about the City. The first residents moved to Bryant Park in 2017; they are continuously improving the neighborhood by adding more amenities and neighborhoods.
When it's complete, the neighborhoods are more than just a group of houses. It will become a vibrant community with residential homes of all types as well as green spaces, parks, and a wide variety of amenities.

## Neighborhoods in Easton Park Austin

As of December 2019, there are currently four neighborhoods in the area.

### 1. Bryant Park

Bryant Park was the first neighborhood of Easton Park. It consisted of 177 lots and is also the central gathering place for the founding homebuyers. The neighborhood represents the Easton Park experience and is one of the pivotal landmarks of lifestyle. Bryant Park is the central landmark of lifestyle that premieres plenty of amenities such as a hammock garden, life-size chess, and Jenga, Ping-Pong and gaming tables. There are also food trucks and open green lawn for events and activities such as festivals and markets. Homes within Bryant Park are sold out.

### 2. Knob Hill

Knob Hill is Easton Park's second residential neighborhood that a lookout point. Explore the gorgeous open lawn or get your heart rate up on the trail. Knob Hill includes 112 lots and 28.29 acres of green spaces that lead to scenic views. The neighborhood is on a gradual slope that rises from the Cottonmouth Creek Restoration Area towards the lookout point near Beacon Knob Way. Homes and lots located in Knob Hill are sold out.

### 3. Union Park

Located in the heart of Easton Park homes for sale, Union Park is divided into two sections. Go East and Adventure West. Go East is home to Easton Park's revamped Experience Park center. It is also home to The Union, the community's 14,000-square-foot amenity center. The Union features a club-style fitness facility, resort-style pool, an event space, gaming room, outdoor fireplace, and more.

In the section of Union Park, Adventure West will bring out everything you love about Austin. From outdoor entertainment options, an integrated trail system that connects to the community garden, amphitheater, pavilion, playground, and green space for yoga and all sorts of events and gatherings. Homes for sale in Union Park start in the low $200s.

### 4. Skyline Park

Along with the 21-acre park, the Skyline Park neighborhood is home to the highest point in the community. The neighborhood has interconnected trails and pocket parks that will allow you to gather and engage with your neighbors and live your lifestyle. Everything you love Austin can be seen within the wonderful neighborhood. The Skyline Park offers homes for sale built by Brookfield Residential, David Weekly Homes, Dream Finders, Pacesetter, Perry Homes, and Taylor Morrison.

Each neighborhood is built by amazing home builders found in Central Texas. There are exceptional home collections and inspiring home designs that will fit your unique style and budget. Homes in the area will vary from large single-family ones to unique houses with shared front gardens.

Parks are the heart and soul of the area whereas there are more than 13 miles of trail systems and 350 acres of pathways for the residents to enjoy. It is projected in the future that there will be more than 6,500 homes and neighborhoods in the area. The area has a variety of outdoor entertainment to make sure you can [turn any past time to a good time](https://www.eastonparkatx.com/amenities/).

## Easton Park Amenities

Easton Park's amenities are like no other. The community's 14,000 square foot amenity center located in Union Park, the heart of the community, offers a resort-style swimming pool, club-style fitness facility, outdoor fireplaces, jam stations, an event place, and more are in store for their current and future residents.

You will find a stunning courtyard, lounge, and bar to hang out with your friends and family. An outdoor amphitheater and community garden are also available in the area. You get the chance to know your neighbors, the amenity center enables the residents to socialize with each other.

Kids will enjoy [Easton Park's Trolley-La!](https://www.eastonparkatx.com/blog/aboard-easton-park-trolley/) which is situated on the highest part of the neighborhood, Knob Hill. It is a great spot for children to play and make friends with. Easton Trolley is a project of the local students from the Creative Action program as has worked with Austin artist, Larissa Stephens. 

Aside from that, there is a trike track located at Bryant Park's Great Lawn where kids can enjoy riding their scooters, bikes, and more. Green spaces within the neighborhood welcome your four-legged friends as well! There are also spots within Easton Park homes for sale where you can host a cookout with your neighbors and families. Don't forget to check out the Hammock Garden if you're looking for a cool place to chill out.
