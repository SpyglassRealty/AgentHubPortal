## Fort Dessau: A Historic Amenity in Northeast Austin

Fort Dessau is a Historical Texas Landmark established in 1873. D.R. Horton has preserved the historical Fort while including beautiful new homes in Pflugerville. The neighborhood took its name from the historic Texas homestead which was established in 1973. ## About the Neighborhood

[Fort Dessau](https://www.drhorton.com/texas/austin/pflugerville/fort-dessau) has easy access for commuters to IH-35, SH45, SH130, and Hwy. 183. Residents and shop and dine at La Frontera, Stone Hill Town Center, and The Domain. It also takes 23 minutes to downtown Austin and about 26 minutes to Bergstrom International Airport.

Plenty of recreational activities are available as well since the community is adjacent to Lake Pflugerville and Park residents can go fishing, swimming, jogging, canoeing, biking, and more. Hiking can be done through Walnut Creek Park and if you would like to tickle your tastebuds with something local, there are restaurants nearby such as Zed's and Chuy's.

Within the area, there are also amenities such as HOA, tot lot, pool, community garden, park, BBQ stations, a splash pad, and a playscape.
