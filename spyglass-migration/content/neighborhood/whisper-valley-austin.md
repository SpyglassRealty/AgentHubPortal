The community is situated within [Manor](https://spyglassrealty.com/manor-real-estate.php), to the east of the new SH-130, and about 20 minutes away from [Downtown Austin](/downtown-austin.php). Residents in the area, especially to those who are working in the tech industry, have convenient access to tech companies such as Dell, Google, and Samsung's corporate campuses that is only about 10 to 20 minutes away. Homes for sale in Whisper Valley Austin are priced from $250s to $400s built and designed by the best builders in Texas.

Whisper Valley is Austin's EcoSmart, Zero Energy community where every home comes with a pre-wired Google Fiber ultra-high-speed internet, Bosch kitchen appliances and ultra-efficient geothermal hear pumps, Nest smart home technology and more. 

Amenities are rich within the area within the 700 acres are beautiful parks, scenic trails, streams, hills, and peaceful meadows and forests. Whisper Valley Austin has plenty of gorgeous natural and recreational areas including their 600-acre park. These include Google powered education centers, geothermal swimming pools, play areas, fitness centers, activity rooms, electric vehicle charging stations, farmers market, and community gardens.

Whisper Valley also offers organic farming and a home food delivery program called Whisper Lettuce which allows homeowners to choose food that is grown on the community's farm.

In the future, more services for the neighborhood will be available such as school campuses, restaurants, transportation centers will be available. This will make it convenient for the residents to access their needs within the proximity of their homes.
