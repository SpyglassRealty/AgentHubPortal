## Neighborhoods Near Barton Hills

Zilker

One of the most desirable neighborhoods in Austin for many locals to settle down is Zilker. The abundant parks, shopping, and dining are within walking distance which provides endless outdoor opportunities for residents and locals residing nearby. During fall, festival enthusiasts unite at the park for Austin City Limits and other music festivals and events held at Zilker Park.

South Lamar

South Lamar is one of the trendiest neighborhoods near Barton Hills Austin providing easy access for residents to delicious locally-owned restaurants, coffee shops, and event venues. The area is rapidly growing due to the convenience it offers to its residents being able to be close proximity to everything they need. From here, you can easily access Barton Springs and Barton Creek Greenbelt where you can swim, hike, relax, and enjoy the amenities around.

Old West Austin

One of the best and [most walkable neighborhoods in Austin](/blog/best-walkable-neighborhoods-in-austin.html) consists of three residential areas; Old Enfield, Pemberton Heights, and Bryker Woods. Old West Austin offers abundant green spaces and convenient access to Peace District Park which are highly appreciated by nature-lovers. Plenty of grocery stores and restaurants are walkable within the neighborhood with Downtown Austin which is just about a 10-minute walk away.

Travis Heights

Travis Heights offers the best of both worlds. Being an area that is highly walkable and close to the shopping and dining district of Austin - South Congress. The neighborhood is known for its eclectic and diverse population composed of students, young professionals, artists, and families. With plenty of outdoor opportunities in the area, getting around the neighborhood is easy whether walking or biking. However, most residents rely on cars to get around.

Hyde Park

Another neighborhood close to Barton Hills homes for sale in [Hyde Park](https://spyglassrealty.com/hyde-park-real-estate.php). An ideal neighborhood perfect for families due to its safety, sense of belonging and top-notch schools in the surrounding area. Most residents live an active lifestyle due to the parks that are within walking distance from each residents' home. Everything is almost accessible for people in Hyde Park where you can find retail stores, bakeries, restaurants, cafes, and more!

## Barton Hills Schools

Barton Hills neighborhood draws many home buyers with school-age children because it has some of the [best and most desirable schools in Austin.](/schools-in-78704.php) Schools in Barton Hills Austin include Barton Hills Elementary, O. Henry Middle School, and Austin High School.

Barton Hills Elementary (K-6) offers academic excellence for kindergarten through sixth grade in a warm and supportive school climate. The school offers strong environmental programs and hands-on science education. Barton Hills Elementary is known for its renowned choir as well as arts and culture programs.

O. Henry Middle School (6-8) offers strong academic programming and a focus on social and emotional learning. O. Henry was named 2012 Schools to Watch by the National Forum to Accelerate Middle-Grade Reform.

Austin High School (9-12) is the oldest continuously operating public school in the state of Texas. Austin High offers more than 250 courses including English, math, social studies, science, STEM, fine arts, humanities, athletics, and world languages.

Preschool options include St. Mark's Episcopal Day School (ages 2-5) and Faith United Methodist Preschool. There are other public magnet school programs and private institutions throughout the area.
