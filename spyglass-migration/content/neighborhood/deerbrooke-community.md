## More About Deerbrooke

Speaking about convenience, the community is minutes away from Hwy 183 and close to everyday work commutes. It also has easy to grocery stores, retail centers, and medical clinics or hospitals. Deerbrooke is placed in a serene, natural space that is great for raising a family or for young professionals. Residents can bring their children to Leander ISD schools which are one of the top-rated schools in Texas.

The area also has access to North Austin Tech Center such as Dell and Apple Campuses.

## Amenities at Deerbrooke

Upscale amenities can be enjoyed that are designed to bring families and neighbors together. The place has a wholesome atmosphere that allows members of the community to make amazing memories in the area. Deerbrooke cultivated spaces that are designed to bring residents together. Here are some of their amenities…

A community center with a flex-use pavilion, entertainment hall, an indoor meeting spaces, and a fitness center. Whether you are in for a summer swim, an event or party to enjoy and celebrate great moments, Deerbrooke's community center got you covered.

Trails are also accessible within the area. There are ample green spaces and a network of wide paths with a trail perfect for hiking and biking plus a gorgeous scenery to view. The breathtaking Texas Hill Country limestone bluffs can be seen from the trails. A perfect place to breathe some fresh air, clear your head, and connect with nature.

When it comes to health and fitness, upscale health and fitness centers are provided. Gyms are fully equipped with high-quality machines, weights, and indoor and outdoor yoga places. Dogs are also welcome in the community. There is a dedicated fenced dog park for your pets to enjoy a game of fetch for a stroll in the park.

It sure gets hot in Texas, an infinity-edge pool is also available with Hill Country views and reclined chairs near the pool's edge.
