## Forest Creek Neighborhood Guide

Forest Creek is a master-planned premier golf course community that is within the City of Round Rock, Texas. The City is one of the fastest-growing metropolitan areas in the nation with highly acclaimed schools plus large employers such as Dell. Homes in Forest Creek can range from 230k to mid-300k and up.

The community is located between I-35 and Hwy. 130 which makes it convenient for its residents to access major roads, dining, shopping, and entertainment centers near the area, it is also just about 30 minutes away from downtown Austin. Forest Creek is also known for its impressive amenities and the Forest Creek Golf Club, the Best Golf Club in Central Texas, with 7,147 yards and par-72. BBQ dinners, community garage sales, and holiday lights contest are some of the few events and activities that are held in Forest Creek Golf Club.

Residents can donate their unused books at Free Little Library and are encouraged to socialize with their neighbors on Spring Carnival. More amenities are provided for residents such as a number of pools to cool off during the hot Texas summer. A playground for kids, a tennis court, and a creek that is perfect for those who want to relax and experience a peaceful afternoon. Living up to its name, the area is surrounded by tall trees and green spaces that will connect you with nature.

## Schools in Forest Creek

The community's schools fall at Round Rock Independent School District. Students in the area will attend the following schools under Round Rock ISD: Forest Creek Elementary, Ridgeview Middle School, and Stony Point High School.
