## About the Brushy Creek Neighborhood

Brushy Creek is located to the north of Austin and to the west of IH-35. Residents enjoy its proximity to local roads and [Downtown Austin](/downtown-austin.php)which is just about 30 minutes away. To its south is Highway 45, West Parmer Lane to its west, and Sam Bass Road and Round Rock Avenue to its east.

Major employers such as Dell and Freescale are found within the Round Rock area as well which makes it easy for its employees residing in Brushy Creek to access their homes within the neighborhood. Residents in the area have easy access to the amenities found within [Round Rock](/teravista-real-estate.php) and [Cedar Park](/cedar-park-real-estate.php). Within Cedar Park, residents can visit the Cedar Park Center, a venue for concerts, rodeos, and various events in the area. Residents can access Twin Creeks as well, a 760-acre residential, country club with its signature 18-hole golf course and water park.

While in Round Rock, the residents can visit the Old Settlers Park, a 645-acre park with sports fields. The Brushy Creek Regional Trail, where residents can bike or bring their dog for a walk. Almost everything can be found within the vicinity of Brushy Creek which makes it convenient for its residents to access everything they need in the neighborhood.
