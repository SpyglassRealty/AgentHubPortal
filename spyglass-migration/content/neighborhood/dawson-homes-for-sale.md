## Dawson Neighborhood Real Estate Search

The Dawson residential neighborhood is located in the middle of an urban setting. The neighborhood maintains its old town character with the home remodeling and modernization in the area. Homes for sale in Dawson south Austin are composed of single-family homes that were primarily built from the 1950's up to the 1980's. There are also townhouses and condos within the area as well. To the east are the roads of South Congress and St. Edwards University while to its north are South Austin's delicious shops, cafes, and nightclubs. For some fun or relaxation, [Barton Creek Greenbelt](/blog/ultimate-guide-to-the-barton-creek-greenbelt-hike-and-bike-trail.html) and [Zilker Park](/blog/zilker-metropolitan-park-guide-events-activities-and-attractions.html) are just to its west!

Dawson is relatively close to plenty of restaurants and coffee shops in the north, but there are also local shops and establishments within the neighborhood. Some of the locals' favorites are Torchy's Tacos, Amy's Ice Cream, Texas Ranch BBQ, and Taqueria La Esperanza. Art galleries and designers reside and run businesses within the area as well. 

The neighborhood is close to Hwy 71 which provides convenient access to the other parts of the city while buses are also available. This makes the neighborhood ideal to settle in due to its proximity to roads and public transportation.
