## About Spanish Oaks

Spanish Oaks is a premier luxury, gated community located in Southwest Austin. The neighborhood is drawn to the gorgeous Hill Country terrain which surrounds the Spanish Oaks Golf Club, one of the top private golf clubs in Texas. The only way to get into the club is to buy a home in the area. Spanish Oaks homes for sale are priced from $300s to over $1 million while custom home typically starts at $1.4 million. The community offers Villas and Garden Homes in its four distinctive neighborhoods.

Spanish Oaks is located off Highway 71 in Southwest Austin with over 1,200 acres with over 396 homes with gorgeous views of the Texas Hill Country. The unique community is known for its cutting edge luxury living with plenty of homes with pool, stunning Hill Country and Barton Creek views, and large lots for outdoor living spaces. Living in Spanish Oaks provides convenient access to shopping and dining areas in [Downtown Austin](/downtown-austin.php) which is just around 20 minutes away as well as [attractions and hotspots in 78704](/all-things-78704.php).

Spanish Oaks real estate range from $300s to over $1 million while custom homes start at $1.4 million. Homes in the community were built from 2004 to present with most lots that range from 3,000 to 8,000. In each and every home contains an elegant kitchen space with high-grade appliances, high ceilings, and rooms with custom built-ins. You'll find yourself in a haven of peace and serenity with homes that overlook the Barton Creek, Little Barton Creek, Spanish Oaks Gold Course, and views of the Hill Country.

## Spanish Oaks Neighborhood

One of the top-rated private golf clubs in Austin is the Spanish Oaks Gold Club where the only way to get in the club is to buy a home in Spanish Oaks. The 18-hole golf course is designed by Bobby Weed and recognized by the Golf Digest as one of the top private golf courses in Texas. In 2019, Spanish Oaks Golf Club is ranked #3 in Texas by Golfweek. The golf club includes practice facilities, activities throughout the year for all ages, and a beautiful clubhouse for gatherings.

There is a wide array of amenities for the residents to enjoy. Bring your children for a fun fishing day at the Fish Camp and spot tadpoles, turtles, and ducklings. Fish Camp is also a place for relaxation for the grown-ups that are looking for a piece of serenity within the private lake. During the hot summer day, the Pool Pavilion is the perfect getaway within Spanish Oaks. The pool includes lap lanes, stone waterfalls, shallow sandy-beaches for the kids to enjoy. Sand volleyball and basketball courts are also available in the area.

Fall in love with the natural beauty of the Hill Country by going on a run or bike around the trails and parks at the beautiful pecan grove. Spanish Oaks also has seven private parks where you can take your family and friends for a picnic along the creek.
