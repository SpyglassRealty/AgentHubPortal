## Lost Creek Neighborhood Amenities

Shopping areas such as the Westlake Village Center and the Barton Creek Square Mall are close to the area. Aside from commercial spaces, Lost Creek residents are close to Barton Creek Greenbelt, which is one of the gems in Austin. It provides 22 acres of hiking trails and a natural swimming hole. Boulder Park is another green space where you can hang out with your friends and family.

An 18 hole championship course can be found in the neighborhood too. Lost Creek Country Club is a private country club that offers social or golf membership to its residents.

## Schools in Lost Creek

Students in Lost Creek attend top-rated schools in the Eanes Independent School District. Schools located in the area are Forest Trail and Barton Creek Elementary Schools for Grades K-5, West Ridge Middle School for Grades 6-8 and Westlake High School for Grades 7-12.
