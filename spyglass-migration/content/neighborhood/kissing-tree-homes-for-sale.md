Kissing Tree is a 55+ community perfectly placed between Austin and San Antonio in the town of San Marcos. Kissing Tree has become one of the most popular communities in Texas to build a home for retirees. The 1,300-acre-gated community offers a unique collection of amenities designed for every residents' lifestyle. It was recognized by the Where to Retire Magazine as one of the best master-planned communities to retire. This active adult community is becoming more popular for homebuyers who are looking for homes that will fit the idea of how they live as well as easy access to essential stores as well as shopping and dining venues.

The community is located atop high ground in San Marcos right off I-35 where the community is surrounded by rivers and natural watering holes. Kissing Tree homes for sale offers three types of homes; Traditional Homes, Villas, and Cottages.

Traditional homes are one- and two-story single-family homes with prices that start in the mid $200s and over 10 available floor plans with two to four bedrooms.

The Strand, a neighborhood within the community, where Villas are situated. Home prices within the neighborhood start in the low $200s with one- to two-story homes and four-floor plans that are from 1-3 bedrooms. These are considered to be low maintenance and close proximity to The Mix, the community's amenity campus.

Lastly, the Cottages, which is located around the 18th hole of the Kissing Tree Golf Course within the area called The Fairway. The Cottages are priced in the high $200s, single-story patio homes with five-floor plans available.

Kissing Tree offers a variety of amenities that includes a 20-acre amenity campus called "The Mix", home to an 18-hole semi-private golf course, 9-hole putting course, 8 pickleball courts, bocce ball, horseshoes, and 18 miles of trail. Located at the center of the community is the Independence Hall, a large social building that is composed of a cafe and a culinary kitchen for gathering and activities.
