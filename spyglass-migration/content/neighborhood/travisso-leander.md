## About Travisso

Travisso is a master-planned community located in the 78641 zip code that was developed in 2014. The community sits on 2,100 acres of land to the north of Lake Travis with 355 acres of natural parkland and 58 acres of regional parkland. Travisso is located near northwest Austin's high tech employment center within Leander ISD. In 2019, Travisso won an award for the Best Community Amenities from the Austin-American Statesman.

Travisso is situated in the rolling hill of Central Texas with stunning views, a 9-acre amenity center life in Travisso is full of fun with new adventures around every corner. The community's award-winning Palazzo Clubhouse, a 9,200 sq ft. of outdoor and indoor recreational space features a resort-style pool, clubhouse, fitness center and serves as a hub of community activities. Nearby shopping and entertainment opportunities are available in the area including the Lakeline Mall, Round Rock Premium Outlets, 1890 Ranch Shopping Center, restaurants, and event venues.

The community is located in Leander, Texas, on the northeast edge of the Texas Hill Country off 183 Tool Road. Leander is one of the fastest-growing and safest city in Texas. Families choose to live in Leander since it was voted #1 best places to raise a family, its affordability, family-oriented events, and education opportunities. Students residing in Leader will attend schools under the Leander Independent School District.
