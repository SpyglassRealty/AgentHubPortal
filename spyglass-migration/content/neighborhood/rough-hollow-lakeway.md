## About the Rough Hollow Neighborhood

More than a community, it's a way of life. That is how the beautiful community of Rough Hollow is described because of year-round activities in the area hosted by the award-winning Rough Life Direction, Rachelle Sturgis. Rough Hollow was developed by Legend Communities, a full-service developer of single-family residential, multi-family residential, active adult communities and associated commercial projects in Central Texas, California, and Colorado with headquarters in Austin, TX.

[Rough Hollow](https://www.roughhollowlakeway.com/) offers world-class amenities such as the exceptional Rough Hollow Yacht Club and Marina where residents can enjoy stunning hill country and waterfront views. It also received an award of 2016 & 2017 MAX awards of Best Unique Feature in a Master-Planned Community. The Highland Village is a water-themed community that offers its residents entertainment and activities.

The community features 22 unique neighborhoods with different styles and sizes from custom or semi-custom built homes. Here are some of the neighborhoods in Rough Hollow:

### 1. The Bluffs

[The Bluffs](https://www.roughhollowlakeway.com/neighborhoods/#the-bluffs) is nestled within the Rough Hollow Cove and features views of Texas Greenbelt and Lake Travis. The neighborhood is the home of the Fall 2010 Parade of Homes™. Homes for sale in The Bluffs start at $690s and ranges from 2,500 to 4,900 square feet. Designed by Westin Homes and Wes Peoples Homes.

### 2. The Summit

The Summit is an exclusive neighborhood that is found on the hilltop of Rough Hollow that offers gorgeous views. Homes in the area are designed by Brookfield Residentials and are priced from mid-$400s.

### 3. Lakeside

Lakeside is a luxurious, contemporary hill country-style subdivision with breathtaking views designed by Drees Homes and Westin Homes. Homes for sale in Lakeside starts at $600s and range from 3,100 to 5,000 sqft.

### 4. West Rin

A growing neighborhood that features canyon views near plenty of outdoor living spaces. Homes for sale in West Rin are designed by Brookfield Residential and Scott Felder Homes.

### 5. Lake Ridge

What was an anticipated gated community is now a part of Rough Hollow. Lake Ridge offers homes that start in $600s and has easy access to Rough Hollow amenities such as the Yacht Club and Marina.

### 6. The Vineyards

[The Vineyards](https://www.roughhollowlakeway.com/neighborhoods/#the-vineyards) at Rough Hollow features the Grandview Collection which is specifically designed with a Tuscan-style influence. If you are looking for luxury Lake Travis living that is affordable, you can finally reach it with the Grandview Collection offered by the Village Builders. Homes in the area start at $330s.

### 7. Highland Terrance

There is a new section that is being constructed in Highland Terrace that will offer greenbelt homesites. Residents in the area will have great views of the greenbelt and the lake. Homes in the Highland Terrance are built by Brookfield Residential and Scott Felder Homes. Home prices start in $400s.

### 8. The Madrones

This beautiful neighborhood is named after the native Madrone trees that surround the greenbelt. Homes for sale in The Madrones starts in $430s and offers magnificent views of the Texas Hill Country and Lake Travis.

### 9. The Peninsula

If you are looking for the most exclusive neighborhood in the area, it's The Peninsula. Luxury custom homes for sale in The Peninsula starts from $1.65 Million while estate-sizes home are at $300s. The neighborhood is located on a private enclave that is overlooking Lake Travis.

### 10. Canyon Pass

Canyon Pass is Rough Hollow's newest neighborhood that offers 66 homesites with a view overlooking the canyons and the Texas Hill Country. Homes are designed-built by Brookfield Residential and Drees Homes. Homes in Canyon Pass start at $360s and sizes range from 1,900 to 3,200 square feet.
