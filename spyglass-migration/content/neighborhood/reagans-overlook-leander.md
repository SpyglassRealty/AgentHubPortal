## About Reagan's Overlook

Reagan's Overlook is one of the brand new communities in Leander. The community is surrounded by the stunning Texas Hill County located to the east of Ronald Reagan Blvd, and to the south of State Hwy 29E. Reagan's Overlook is currently being developed by top hoe builders such as Highland Homes, Drees Homes, Brohn Homes, and Jimmy Jacobs.

Reagan's Overlook features estate homes on 1-acre homesites with traditional and contemporary elevations in stone and stucco and styles from traditional styles to modern homes with a touch of luxury. Houses within the community range from 3,200 square feet up to 4,100 square feet with one- or two-story options and a gorgeous backyard perfect for gatherings. The community is served by the Leander Independent School District, one of the top-rated schools in Texas.

Residents can enjoy various amenities such as hiking t the Southwest Williamson County Regional Park, shopping, dining, and entertainment options. Reagan's Overlook is located in Leander which is 30 miles from northwest Austin. Leander is one of the fastest-growing cities in the nation offering panoramic views and abundant space for outdoor open space recreation. The developments in transportation, employment, and housing have made Leander grow and become one of the most desirable places to raise a family.
