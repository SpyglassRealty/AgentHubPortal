## Something About the Galindo Neighborhood

Galindo is a neighborhood that features homes that were built in the 50's and '60s as well as newly built modernized homes for sale. The neighborhood is within [South Austin](/south-austin.php) although compared to the nearby areas of [Zilker](/zilker-homes-and-condos.php), [Barton Hills](/barton-hills.php), and [Travis Heights](/travis-heights-real-estate.php), Galindo offers a lower price tag for its homes. The neighborhood retains its vintage character as isn't that much known compared to the other [neighborhoods in 78704](/neighborhoods-in-78704.php).

From trendy shops, delicious restaurant lanes, iconic homegrown coffee shops, live music, and clubs, the residents of Galindo enjoy the benefits within the South Austin neighborhood. [South Lamar](/south-lamar-homes-for-sale.php) and South Congress are nearby where you can hang out with your friends or on your own. Galindo is about 11 minutes away from [Zilker Park](/blog/zilker-metropolitan-park-guide-events-activities-and-attractions.html), 10 minutes away from Downtown Austin and less than 20 minutes from the Bergstrom International Airport.

The only busy street within the area is South First St. while the entire neighborhoods are peaceful. South Austin Park and Recreation Center are nearby where multiple sports events are being held as well as recreational venues for children and adults.
