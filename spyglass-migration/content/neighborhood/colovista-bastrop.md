## About Colovista Bastrop

Colovista Bastrop is a golf community situated in the Lost Pines of Bastrop. Colovista was the vision of Les Apelt who built a 17,000 square feet mansion overlooking the Colorado River. The Mansion then became a destination for special events and real estate development included lots and golf courses.

Colovista homes are west of County Roads 191 and 192 in Bastrop, part of Bastrop County and the 78602 zip code. Residents conveniently connect to Austin, and the rest of Central Texas via Texas State Highway 95, Highway 71, and Texas State Highway 21. Colovista homes for sale are 8 miles from Bastrop Medical Center, 6 miles from Bastrop State Park, and 8 miles from Main Street.

Colovista is just a few miles outside Bastrop where you can find shopping, dining, and entertainment options as well as parks and golf courses. Lake Bastrop is also at reach where you can fish, boat, or kayak.
