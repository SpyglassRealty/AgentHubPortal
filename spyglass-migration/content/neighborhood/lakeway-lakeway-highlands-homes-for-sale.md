## Proximity to Lake Travis and Access to Hiking and Biking Trails

Nestled in the picturesque rolling hills of West Austin, the Lakeway Highlands area is famous for its proximity to Lake Travis and access to scenic hiking and biking trails. Anyone who loves outdoor activities, swimming, kayaking, and fishing will find this neighborhood an absolute dream. The community is also the perfect place to relax in a tranquil and serene environment surrounded by Texas Hill Country ambiance.

Here are some bullet points to highlight the key features of the Lakeway Highlands area:

- Breathtaking views of Lake Travis and surrounding Texas Hill Country

- Easy access to hiking and biking trails

- Swimming, fishing, and kayaking opportunities in Lake Travis

- Proximity to some of Austin's best golf courses
