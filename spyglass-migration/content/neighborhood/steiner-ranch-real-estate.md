## About Steiner Ranch

Steiner Ranch is one of the best places to live in Austin. You'll never run out of things to do with multiple perks and amenities in the area. Convenience, privacy, community, lifestyle, and social life is what Steiner Ranch embodies making it one of the places great for families and for the young and old to live in.

Nature enthusiasts will enjoy the environment of green spaces. The community has dedicated 1,500 square feet to wildlife habitats, greenbelts, and parks. Because of this dedication of preserving the environment, it has been recognized by the Austin Business Journal as the Best Master-planned community in 2004.

## Steiner Ranch Amenities

Steiner Ranch's main entrance is located at RR 620 at Quinlan Park Road which is just minutes away from the Mansfield dam and close to Lakeway. Transportation in the area is easy making it convenient for its residents. You may able to walk around and reach some of the amenities in the neighborhood although the area is large, you might also need a car to go around. Capital Metro's Route 122 stops near the Four Point Shopping center near the neighborhood and another at 3M.

There are private amenities in the area such as a private membership in Lake Club on Lake Austin, basketball and tennis courts, swimming pools. There are also 12 miles of nature trails, 19 marinas, 15 public parks and 10 golf courses in the area wherein active residents can keep conveniently keep up with their lifestyle and love for nature.

## Schools in Steiner Ranch

Students in Steiner Ranch will attend high acclaimed schools within the Leander Independent School District. For elementary schools, students can attend Steiner Ranch Elementary, River Ridge Elementary, Laura Welch Bush Elementary. Canyon Ridge Middle School for students in middle school.

These schools are located within Steiner Ranch. For high school students, a brand new high school opened, Vandegrift High School which is just located minutes away from the neighborhood.
