## About Butler Ranch Estates

Butler Ranch Estates is a new home development by Century Communities in Dripping Springs, TX. The community is located 24 miles southwest of Downtown Austin in Dripping Springs' 78620 zip code. Residents enjoy the quiet and peaceful setting the community has to offer.
Butler Ranch Estates' construction started back in 1994 and has developed throughout the years. Homes for sale in Butler Ranch Estates are mostly single-family homes with a wide variety of styles to choose from that will suit your lifestyle. Most houses sit on quarter-acre lots for additional privacy with 3-5 bedrooms, large walk-in showers and closets, high-end kitchens, covered patios, and beautiful exterior designs.
The community is located in Dripping Springs, the Gateway to the Texas Hill Country and the wedding capital of Texas, where residents can enjoy several retail and dining options. Outdoor enthusiasts will love walking at the trails at Charro Ranch Park. For more outdoor fun, beat the heat at the Hamilton Pool Preserve where you can hike, swim, picnic, or admire the 50-foot waterfall that will reconnect you with nature. Dripping Springs is an oasis for families that are looking for a place that is close to the hustle and bustle of the big city.
