## Cherry Creek Real Estate Search

Cherry Creek is a residential subdivision located at Southwest Austin. The neighborhood was developed during the '70s to '90s. Residents enjoy the convenient and secluded area of Cherry Creek. Even though the neighborhood is secluded, residents are able to easily access hotspots at Downtown Austin and the [South Congress neighborhood](/austin-south-congress-homes-for-sale.php). Public transportation is also available near the area that can take you around Austin.

This page is updated with Cherry Creek home listings several times per day directly from the Austin, Texas MLS. Each Cherry Creek MLS listing includes the property price, days on market, square footage, year built, lot size, number of bedrooms and bathrooms, assigned schools, construction type, and other important details as entered by the listing agent in the MLS.

Also review local Cherry Creek property tax information and the current listing status (active, under contract, or pending). As available, numerous property features such as greenbelt locations, views, swimming pools and Cherry Creek neighborhood amenities including parks and golf courses will be listed.

## Cherry Creek Things to Do

Cherry Creek has plenty of local shops, dining areas, entertainment venues, and amenities that residents can enjoy. The neighborhood association is active and encourages the residents to join events for a sense of belonging for each and everyone living in the area.
There are plenty of amenities in the area where the residents can enjoy relaxing, hiking, biking and even swimming. [Mary Moore Searight Park](https://www.austintexas.org/listings/mary-moore-searight-metropolitan-park/5747/) and Dick Nichols Park are some of the nearby ones where you can bring your dog for a walk. Beat the hot Texas heat by taking a dip at the Stephenson Preserve and for golf enthusiasts, you can visit the Grey Rock Golf Club for a game of golf with your neighbors and friends.
