In just a few minutes, you will be able to reach Lake Travis, another stunning natural lake with recreational opportunities for everyone. Here are some activities to enjoy and experience with your family, friends, or significant other in the area.

Can you swim in Lake Austin? Swimming will definitely be on top of your list. According to [AustinTexas.gov](https://www.austintexas.gov/page/lake-austin), No person shall swim or float beyond 50 feet of the Lake Austin or Lake Long shoreline. Although this does not apply to officially designated swimming areas. Once you live within the area, get easy access to the lake for a quick dip by staying at a hotel, resort, or vacation house. Boat rentals, kayaks, and paddleboards are available to explore the 1,599 acres of water. You can also go on a boat cruise since there are boat tours in the area that focuses on the scenic views of the lake.

Residents in the area can easily access several parks where they can hike and bike. Explore the parks near Lake Austin homes for sale such as The Turkey Creek Trail, a 2.8-mile trail that features a river and is easy to hike at for kids. Panther Hollow Trail has a canyon-like climb if you are looking for more adventure. There are also nearby parks and recreation centers such as the Emma Long Recreational Park and Selma Hughes Park.

While you're here, check out [Barton Springs Pool](/blog/barton-springs-pool-review.html) within the [Zilker Metropolitan Park](/blog/zilker-metropolitan-park-guide-events-activities-and-attractions.html). ## Schools near Lake Austin

Nestled along the serene shores of Lake Austin, the Lake Austin Waterfront community is not only renowned for its picturesque views and luxurious homes but also for its proximity to some of the top educational institutions in the region. Families residing in this area are fortunate to have a plethora of schooling options, ensuring that their children receive the best education tailored to their needs.

Public School Districts:

- Eanes Independent School District (Eanes ISD): Recognized for its academic excellence, Eanes ISD has consistently been ranked among the top school districts in Texas. The district boasts a rich curriculum, state-of-the-art facilities, and a dedicated team of educators committed to nurturing the next generation of leaders. Students in Eanes ISD benefit from a holistic educational experience that emphasizes both academic and extracurricular achievements.

- Leander Independent School District (Leander ISD): Serving a diverse student population, Leander ISD is known for its innovative teaching methods and commitment to student success. With a focus on technology integration, hands-on learning, and community engagement, Leander ISD ensures that its students are well-prepared for the challenges of the 21st century.

- Lake Travis Independent School District (Lake Travis ISD): A beacon of educational distinction, Lake Travis ISD offers a comprehensive educational program that caters to the varied needs of its students. From advanced placement courses to specialized programs for gifted students, Lake Travis ISD is dedicated to fostering an environment where every student can thrive.

Private Schooling Options:

For families who are considering private education, Lake Austin Waterfront is in close proximity to several esteemed private institutions. These schools offer specialized curriculums, smaller class sizes, and individualized attention, ensuring that students receive a unique and tailored educational experience. Whether you're looking for faith-based education, Montessori methods, or a school with a particular academic focus, the Lake Austin area has a variety of options to cater to your child's specific needs.

The Lake Austin Waterfront community is truly a haven for families prioritizing education. With a range of top-tier public and private schools nearby, residents can rest assured that their children will have access to the best educational opportunities available.
