## Northwest Hills Homes - Austin TX

Welcome to your local Northwest Hills homes for sale & [real estate in West Austin](/west-austin-homes-for-sale.php) resource. Northwest Hills is scenic and has an unexpected appeal to green spaces. The neighborhood is surrounded by several major highways such as Hwy 1 or MoPac, Anderson Lane, and Hwy 360 which gives residents easier access to entertainment, shopping, dining areas. Downtown Austin is only seven miles away, high-end retail shops at the Domain are five miles away. While the Arboretum is less than five miles away.

Some of the well-known green spaces in the area are [Bull Creek](https://austinparks.org/bull-creek/), Bright Leaf Preserve, and Mount Bonnell. These are must-see, must-visit protected areas and natural landmarks you will fall in love with. Single-family homes and condos sprawl in Northwest Hills. Some of the subdivisions in the area are Cat Mountain and Westover Hills.

## Schools in Northwest Hills

Students in Northwest Hills attend schools in the Austin Independent School District (Austin ISD). Schools located in the area are Doss, Highland Park, and Hill Elementary Schools. Lamar, Murchison Middle Schools, and Anderson and McCallum High Schools.
