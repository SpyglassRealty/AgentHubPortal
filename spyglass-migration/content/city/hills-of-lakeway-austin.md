# Hills of Lakeway

The Hills of Lakeway, also known as The Hills or The Village of The Hills, is a gated community located at the heart of Lakeway with magnificent views of the Texas Hill Country and Lake Travis. The well-established neighborhood consists of over 600 homes with gorgeous views of the Hill Country and the golf course. Hills of Lakeway homes for sale start in the mid $200s to over a million while floor plans range from 2000 to over 6000 square feet.

## About The Hills of Lakeway

The Hills of Lakeway is located just about 22 miles from [Downtown Austin](/downtown-austin.php) and 27 miles from the Bergstrom International Airport. The construction of this guard-gated community started in 1982 through 2011 with custom homes that are unique in character and a peaceful escape located in the heart of Lakeway. New homes are continuously being built within the community but you can also find homes that were built during the '80s.

The neighborhood was originally within the extraterritorial jurisdiction of the City of Austin then in May 1997, the Hills of Lakeway became an incorporated city in Texas. Residents get to have the exclusive address of "The Hills, Texas 78738" as designated by the US Post Office.

An average home in The Hills is 3,600 square feet with 3-5 beds and 4-6 bathrooms. Most of The Hills of Lakeway real estate consists of details such as hardwood floorings, high ceilings, and elegant kitchens. The Hills of Lakeway is truly a wonderful neighborhood to settle in with the advantages of having close proximity to [Lake Travis](/lake-travis-real-estate.php), green spaces, and the dynamic area of Downtown Austin.

## Things To Do in The Hills of Lakeway

The Hills Country Club offers its members first-class amenities like the two championship golf courses: The Hills and Flintrock Falls. These courses are Nicklaus-designed and are surrounded by the stunning scenery of the Texas Hill Country. The country club is home to the world-recognized Academy of Golf, featuring a 500-yeard practice center with target greens and championship golf holes.

Another amenity located within the country club is The World of Tennis which features 26 outdoor tennis courts with 2 indoor courts and a recreational swimming pool. A fitness facility and day spa are being added to the complex. For gathering and events, there is also a sports bar and grill within The Hills Country Club making it a worthwhile experience for its residents. Lake Travis is just within the vicinity of The Hills, about more or less 15 minutes, where residents can enjoy the clear water for kayaking, boating, or relaxing near the waters.

About 10 minutes away, residents can reach The Hill Country Galleria where over 100 shops and restaurants are available. If residents are looking for a fun nightlife in downtown Austin, the travel is about 30 minutes away from The Hills.
