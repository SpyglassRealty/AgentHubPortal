## Something About Manor, Texas

Manor is a small town named after its original Postmaster James B. Manor. It is located 12 miles to the northeast of [downtown Austin](/downtown-austin.php) with a population that is over 9,300. The city of Manor has a total area of 1.1 square miles with a wide array of amenities and recreational areas for its residents. Today, Manor is one of the fastest-growing suburbs in the Austin-Round Rock Metropolitan Area.

Residents will enjoy plenty of amenities and activities. Jennie Lane Park, located in Manor's Historical Downtown, serves as one of the popular parks for community gatherings and events. This includes events such as the Manor Farmers Market, Manor's Summertime Movie Series, and Christmas in the park. Today, the park is also a "Smart Park" due to its WiFi access and coded signages which can be accessed through smartphones to know more information about the history of the neighborhood. Jennie Lane Park consists of a pavilion, outdoor exercise equipment, and a gazebo.

Manor is also home to East Metro Park, a recreational park with multiple amenities such as soccer fields, basketball courts, hiking trails, playscapes, and a pavilion you can rent for your events. There is also a swimming pool within the park which is just five minutes away from the southeast of Manor. ShadowGlen is a subdivision is Manor that offers plenty of amenities as well which includes a four-acre water park and a junior Olympic-size pool.
