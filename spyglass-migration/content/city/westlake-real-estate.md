## What You Need to Know About Westlake Hills

West Lake Hills is a suburb in Austin and is also a town with its own city police and fire department and commissions. The area is greatly desired due to its calm location which is wooded with plenty of trees, shrubs, and plants. It also offers pleasing views of the hill country and the convenience of going in and out of the neighborhood.

On the eastern edge of Westlake is MoPac and extends west to Highway 71. The southern boundary is the Colorado River and the Barton Creek to the north. If you chose to be in the Davenport Ranch, you'll be close to the Austin Country Club which consists of its perfectly manicured golf course, tennis courts, marina, and fine dining.

There is also have easy access to Loop 360 if you plan to go North or South. Two elementary schools and a high school can be found across Loop 360. The neighborhood is also close to another country club which is Lost Creek Country Club. It also has a golf course, pool, and tennis courts.

Westlake also offers you the opportunity to live and admire the lovely view of Lake Austin's waterfront. A quick trip across the Colorado River makes is Tarrytown and the University of Texas. There are also treks down Capital of Texas Highway or the Bee Cave Road that leads to the South of Austin and beyond.

## West Lake Hills Neighborhood

Home to a population of 3,400 residents, the neighborhood offers plenty of amenities to enjoy such as the outdoors and the natural scenery. It is adjacent to the Wild Basin Wilderness and close to Mayfield Park and Natural Preserve. If you think that's not enough, the Zilker Park and Barton Creek are not far away!

All residents work together to preserve the area's environment and take pride in its natural beauty. Residents in the area also have a sense of community, belonging since they are a small, united, largely independent neighborhood. There are many local businesses, stores, and eateries around the neighborhood. And if you are looking for more places to dine, shop, and nightlife, Downtown Austin is just a 10-minute drive away.

Whether if you are new to the neighborhood or a resident that is coming back to the town, the friendliness of the locals will make you feel like you are home.

## Schools

The neighborhood is part of the top-rated Eanes Independent School District or Eanes ISD. For residents with children living with them, finding a top-notch school wouldn't be hard. There 10 schools in the area which includes Eanes Elementary School, Bridge Point Elementary School (PK-5), Barton Creek Elementary (K-5), Cedar Creek Elementary School (K-5), Forest Trail Elementary School (K-5), Hill Country Middle School (5-8), West Ridge Middle School (6-8), and Westlake High School (9-12), and more.

As on 2018-2019, Eanes ISD is serving 8, 075 students in the area.
