## About Cedar Park

Cedar Park was incorporated in 1973 with a council-manager system of local government. Cedar Park Austin homes for sale are considered as a suburb in the Austin Metro. It is located minutes from Round Rock, Leander, Austin, and the Texas Hill Country.

There are many great neighborhoods in Cedar Park such as [Forest Oaks](/cedar-park-forest-oaks-homes-for-sale.php), [Creekview](/cedar-park-creekview-homes-for-sale.php), Hidden Valley Estates, [Brushy Creek](/brushy-creek-real-estate.php), and more. Cedar Park homes for sale is also home to the [Twin Creeks](/cedar-park-twin-creeks-homes-for-sale.php)home development. Twin Creeks is a 760-acre residential, country club, a Fred Couple-designed signature 18-hole golf course, and water park. Twin creeks was the first private country club in Cedar Park located three miles south of Highway 183 at the intersection of Anderson Mill Road and Cypress Creek Road.
