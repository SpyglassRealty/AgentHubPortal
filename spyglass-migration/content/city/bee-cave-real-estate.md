## About the Bee Cave Neighborhood

About 12 miles away from [Downtown Austin](/downtown-austin.php) is a neighborhood named after a large cave of wild bees in the area, Bee Cave. Bee Cave is located at the West of Austin. It is known for its beautiful natural scenery without being too far from the city. Residents in the area have easy access to Hwy 71 and accessibility to public transportation. Bee Cave is one of the fastest-growing rural community that offers a wide variety of neighborhoods for homebuyers.

Bee Cave is 20-30 minutes away from the dynamic area of Downtown Austin, but there are also plenty of activities that can be found within the city that you can enjoy. Whether you are looking for local shops, restaurants, swimming holes, lakes, and entertainment venues, residents in Bee Cave has easy access to all of those amenities.

If you are looking for a place to cool down during the hot Texas summer, Hamilton Pool, Lakeway Swim Center, and [Lake Travis](/lake-travis-real-estate.php) are some of the places you can visit! Golf enthusiasts will enjoy spending time at the Hills of Lakeway Country Club, Spanish Oaks Gold Club, or the Falconhead Golf Club. Bring your pet for a walk at the Bee Cave Central Park or the Pace Bend Park. There are also shopping centers about the area such as the Hill Country Galleria.
