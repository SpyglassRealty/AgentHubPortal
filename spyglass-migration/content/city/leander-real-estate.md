## About Leander Real Estate

Leander, Texas is now one of the fastest-growing cities in the United States. It is located 25 miles north of [Downtown Austin](/downtown-austin.php) within the Travis and Williamson counties. Leander is rich with panoramic views and abundant green spaces for wildlife. There are over 40 acres of space dedicated for recreation such as hiking, biking, and various outdoor sports.

Leander grew over the past years due to the development of land that has resulted in an influx of newcomers into the suburbs. With developments in master-planned communities, transportation, and employment opportunities, Leander is one of the ideal places for those looking for homes with a small-town feel yet close to the big city.
