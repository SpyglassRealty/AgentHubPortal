## What Got Built

### 🛡️ 1. Agent Retention Risk Dashboard
**Impact: Early warning system to prevent costly agent departures**

Built at `/admin/dashboards/retention-risk` in Mission Control.

**The Problem It Solves:**
- Agent attrition is the silent killer of brokerage revenue
- One top producer leaving can mean $5M-$20M+ in lost volume
- By the time you notice, it's usually too late — they've already signed elsewhere
- No existing tool in Mission Control flagged agents at risk of leaving

**How It Works:**
A risk scoring engine (0-100) that analyzes every active roster member using 7 weighted signals:

| Signal | Severity | What It Detects |
|--------|----------|-----------------|
| Production Cliff | Critical | Volume dropped 50%+ quarter-over-quarter |
| Declining Velocity | Warning | Volume down 25-50% QoQ |
| Extended Inactivity | Critical | No closes in 180+ days |
| Growing Silence | Warning | 90-180 days since last close |
| Empty Pipeline | Critical | No pending deals or active listings |
| Key Producer at Risk | Critical | Top producer showing decline signals |
| Post-Cap Slowdown | Warning | Hit cap but production declining (classic pre-departure pattern) |
| Struggling New Agent | Warning | Joined 90+ days ago, ≤1 deals closed |
| Year 2-3 Plateau | Info | Mid-tenure agent with flat/modest production |

**Dashboard Features:**
- **KPI Row:** Active agents, critical/high risk counts, total volume at risk, roster health score
- **Watchlist Tab:** Top 5 highest-impact at-risk agents (sorted by volume), expandable with full metrics and AI-generated recommended actions
- **Analysis Tab:** Risk distribution pie chart, signal frequency bar chart, Volume vs Risk scatter plot (identifies high-value retention targets in top-right quadrant)
- **All Agents Tab:** Full roster with search, filter by risk level, sort by risk score or volume, expandable detail cards

**The Recommendation Engine:**
Each agent gets a contextual, plain-English recommendation based on their specific risk signals. Examples:
- "Schedule a 1:1 coffee with [name] this week. This agent represents 3.2% of your volume..."
- "Something changed for [name] — production dropped significantly. Check in to understand why..."
- "New agent [name] needs more support to get traction. Pair them with a mentor..."

**Technical:**
- 100% client-side computation — uses existing Xano hooks (roster, transactions, listings, pending)
- Zero new API routes needed
- ~1,085 lines of new code
- Integrated into sidebar navigation under "GROWTH" section

### ⚔️ 2. Recruiting Battle Cards
**Impact: Competitive intelligence tool that arms recruiters for every conversation**

Built at `/admin/dashboards/recruiting-battlecards` in Mission Control.

**The Problem It Solves:**
- When recruiting from Compass, KW, eXp, RE/MAX, or Real (direct), you need to know their economics, weaknesses, and how to handle objections
- This information was scattered across documents and brain files
- No quick-reference tool existed for in-the-moment recruiting conversations

**How It Works:**
Select a competitor → get a full battle card with:

1. **Side-by-side Economics:** Spyglass vs competitor (split, cap, fees, rev share, stock)
2. **Financial Scenarios:** Cost comparison at 4 production levels ($100K-$500K GCI), showing exact dollar savings at Spyglass
3. **Talking Points:** Competitor pain points + Spyglass advantages
4. **Objection Handlers:** 3-4 common objections per competitor with persuasive, specific responses
5. **Target Profiles:** Ideal agent profiles to recruit from each brokerage
6. **Tech Stack Comparison:** Spyglass's 10 tools vs competitor's typically 3-4
7. **Copy to Clipboard:** One-click battle card summary for quick sharing

**Competitors Covered:**
- **Compass** — Post-merger chaos, corporate culture, no equity
- **Keller Williams** — 6% royalty, high cap, dated tech
- **eXp Realty** — Higher total cost than Spyglass, culture fatigue, no local brand
- **RE/MAX** — Crushing desk fees ($24-36K/yr), no equity
- **Real (Direct)** — Same economics but no custom tools or local community

**Key Data Points Baked In:**
- Compass-Anywhere merger (Jan 2026) creating agent displacement
- Real Brokerage: #5 nationally, $36.4M cash, zero debt
- Spyglass advantage: 10 tech tools vs 3-4 at most competitors
- Revenue share math: 5/4/3/2/1% willable model vs competitors' inferior or nonexistent programs

**Technical:**
- ~931 lines of new code
- Pure frontend — all competitive data hardcoded for speed
- Integrated financial scenario calculator
- Copy-to-clipboard battle card export
- Nav: GROWTH section in sidebar
