## What Got Built

### üöÄ Growth Intelligence Dashboard (THE main deliverable)
**Impact: Ryan's new strategic nerve center for the $1B goal**

Built a comprehensive admin dashboard page at `/admin/dashboards/growth` in Mission Control that answers every question a brokerage owner asks at 2am:

**Four tabs of intelligence:**

#### üìä Pace Tab
- **Cumulative Volume vs $1B Goal** ‚Äî Area chart showing actual YTD volume against the goal line. Instantly see if you're above or below pace.
- **Monthly Volume** ‚Äî Bar chart with reference line showing what's needed per month
- **Monthly Transactions** ‚Äî Bar chart with GCI line overlay
- **Pace Summary Cards** ‚Äî Year progress %, YTD volume, monthly needed, projected annual

#### üë• Agents Tab
- **Agent Production Tiers** ‚Äî Four-tier system (Elite/Strong/Developing/Inactive) with volume, GCI, brokerage revenue, and avg deals per tier
- **Tier Distribution Pie Chart** ‚Äî Visual of where volume comes from
- **Revenue Concentration Risk** ‚Äî Top 10% and Top 20% contribution percentages, plus Gini coefficient (measures inequality of production distribution)
- **Risk Assessment** ‚Äî Automated warning if too much revenue depends on too few agents

#### üí∞ Economics Tab
- **Per-Agent Economics** ‚Äî Annualized avg volume, GCI, brokerage revenue, and deals per agent
- **Scenario: 300 Agents at Current Productivity** ‚Äî Projects what happens if we hit 300 agents with today's avg productivity. Does it hit $1B? Shows projected volume, GCI, brokerage revenue, and deals.
- **What's Needed to Hit $1B at 300 Agents** ‚Äî Required volume/deals/GCI per agent vs current averages
- **Monthly Brokerage Revenue** ‚Äî Chart showing GCI vs agent payouts vs what Spyglass keeps
- **Strategy Insight** ‚Äî "Recruit productive agents AND coach developing agents up. Both levers matter."

#### üèÜ Leaderboard Tab
- **Top 25 Producers** ‚Äî Ranked by volume with tier badges, GCI, deals, avg price, listing/buyer split, brokerage revenue contribution, and volume share %
- **Medal styling** ‚Äî Gold/Silver/Bronze for top 3
- **Volume share bars** ‚Äî Visual indicator of each agent's contribution to total volume

**Top KPI Row (always visible):**
- Volume ‚Üí $1B progress bar with YoY comparison
- Agents ‚Üí 300 progress (need X more, ~Y/month target)
- Pipeline (pending deals volume + GCI)

## Market Research: Austin Housing Market ‚Äî February 2026

### Key Data Points (from ABoR, KXAN, Redfin, local analysts):
- **Median sold price (Austin metro):** $400,495 (Jan 2026, down 2.3% YoY per KXAN)
- **Median sold price (City of Austin):** ~$500K per Redfin (down 3.6% YoY)
- **Days on market:** 88 days (highest since March 2011)
- **Active listings:** 10,372 across metro
- **Months of inventory:** 4.2 (balanced market territory)
- **Price cuts:** 53%+ of listings took price reductions
- **Mortgage rates:** ~6.1% (eased from peak)
- **Homestead exemption:** Jumped to $140K (200K for 65+)

### Market Bottom Forecast:
- Most analysts project bottom in Q2-Q3 2026
- Prices expected to stabilize in $430K-$440K range
- 2027: Modest 2-4% annual appreciation expected
- Job market: 3.4% unemployment, 1.6% employment growth

### What This Means for Spyglass:
1. **Agent pitch is strong:** "In a 88-DOM market, sellers NEED real support. That's what we provide."
2. **Buyer agents have more value now:** Full inspection periods, price negotiations, seller concessions. Buyers need skilled agents to navigate this.
3. **Recruiting opportunity:** Agents at big brokerages are seeing less support in a tougher market. Boutique + tech = compelling pitch.
4. **Volume pressure:** Lower prices mean more transactions needed for the same volume. The $1B goal requires ~2,500 transactions at $400K avg.
5. **Pipeline is key:** With 88 DOM, pipeline visibility matters more than ever. More deals are "in progress" at any given time.
