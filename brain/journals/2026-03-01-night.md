### 2. Agent Production Planner ✅
**Location:** `projects/agent-production-planner/index.html`  
**Commit:** `dfbc8a01a`

Every agent should know their numbers. This tool reverse-engineers an income goal into daily activity targets. It's both a productivity tool for existing agents AND a recruiting differentiator ("We don't just hand you a desk — we help you build a business plan").

**Features:**
- **Income goal input** → auto-calculates deals needed at Spyglass (accounts for split + cap)
- **Avg sale price, commission rate, deals closed YTD** — all configurable
- **5 dashboard KPIs:** Total deals needed, remaining, monthly target, weekly target, pace status
- **Monthly deal tracker** — click +/- to log closings per month, with seasonality-adjusted targets (Austin RE patterns)
- **Cumulative pace chart** — actual vs. target line, instant visual of ahead/behind
- **Activity reverse-engineering** — configurable conversion rates (lead→appt, appt→contract, contract→close) → exact leads/week, appointments/week, calls/day
- **Business economics panel** — GCI, brokerage fees, expenses, tax reserve → true take-home
- **Pipeline health check** — active listings, pending, buyers, nurturing leads → coverage ratio + health score
- **Cumulative income projection chart**
- **Motivational quotes** rotated randomly

**The value proposition:** This turns a vague "I want to make $200K" into "You need 15 leads per week, 3 appointments, and 1.5 closings per month." Agents love this clarity.

**How to use:**
- Open in any browser — zero dependencies, single HTML file
- Ideal for: onboarding new agents, quarterly business planning sessions, accountability check-ins
- Pair with the Commission Calculator: "Here's how much you'll keep, and here's exactly how to get there"
