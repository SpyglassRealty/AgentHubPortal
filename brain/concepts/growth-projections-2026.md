## The Math

### Agent Growth Required
- **Net gain needed:** 121 agents (179 → 300)
- **Typical annual attrition:** ~15-20% in real estate
- **Estimated agents leaving:** ~30-35 over 12 months
- **Gross hires needed:** ~155 agents in 2026
- **Monthly recruiting rate:** ~13 agents/month

### Revenue Math
- To hit $1B at current avg deal size ($385K): need **2,597 transactions**
- At 8.7 deals/agent avg: need **299 agents** (checks out)
- But NOT all agents are equal:
  - Top 20% of agents: ~15+ deals/year
  - Middle 60%: 5-10 deals/year  
  - Bottom 20%: 1-4 deals/year (part-time, new agents)

### Realistic Production Model

| Tier | % of Agents | Agents | Avg Deals | Total Deals |
|------|------------|--------|-----------|-------------|
| Top Producers (15+ deals) | 20% | 60 | 20 | 1,200 |
| Mid Producers (5-14 deals) | 50% | 150 | 8 | 1,200 |
| New/Part-time (1-4 deals) | 30% | 90 | 3 | 270 |
| **TOTAL** | 100% | 300 | **8.9 avg** | **2,670** |

At $385K avg: **2,670 × $385K = $1.03B** ✅

### Key Insight
The path to $1B is NOT just about getting to 300 agents — it's about the MIX. Recruiting mid-to-top producers (Beacon's sweet spot of 15-30 deals/year agents) is 5x more valuable than adding new agents.

**One agent doing 20 deals = Seven new agents doing 3 deals each**

## Houston Growth Opportunity

Houston is the untapped lever. Currently only 27 agents in a metro of 7M+ people.

| Metric | Austin | Houston | Opportunity |
|--------|--------|---------|-------------|
| Current Agents | 152 | 27 | +125 headroom |
| Competition Intensity | Very High | High | Less saturated for boutique |
| Avg Deal Size | $450K | $350K | Lower but more volume available |
| Market Maturity | Mature | Growth phase | Easier to recruit |

**Strategy:** Aim for 50+ Houston agents by EOY (from 27). That's almost half the net growth target from one market.

## Revenue Per Agent Levers

Instead of just adding bodies, increase revenue per agent:

1. **SOI Touchpoint System** → +1-2 deals/agent/year from past clients
2. **Lead Gen (PPC/SEO)** → Company leads distributed to productive agents
3. **Pulse Market Reports** → Agents share, generate inbound leads
4. **Better onboarding** → New agents productive faster (3 months vs 6)
5. **Transaction support** → Agents spend less time on paperwork, more on deals

**If avg deals/agent goes from 7.3 → 10:** Only need 260 agents for $1B, not 300.
