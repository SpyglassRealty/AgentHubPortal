## TL;DR
Spring selling season kicks off with Austin in a textbook buyer's market — 4.2 months inventory, 69 DOM, median ~$400K metro / ~$500K city. But the headline is what's happening *beneath* the surface: $15.7B in agent production shifted brokerages nationwide last year, agent turnover hit 6.8% (up from 6.0%), and the "Great Agent Reshuffle" is accelerating. This is THE recruiting window. Rates are expected to ease to the 5.5-6% range by late 2026, which means the agents who are hurting now will be thriving in 6 months — recruit them while they're searching.

## 3. The Great Agent Reshuffle — By the Numbers

### Recruiting Insight's 2026 Report (184,097 agents analyzed)
- **$15.7 billion** in annual production shifted between brokerages
- **6.8% annual turnover** (up from 6.0%) — acceleration, not slowdown
- **~350 productive agents per MLS per month** are changing brokerages
- **Internal brand transfers:** $1.516M avg production, 89% retention
- **External recruits:** $1.218M avg production, 76% retention
- **Top 20% of agents now control 80%+ of closed volume** in many MLSs

### What's Driving Movement
1. **Transaction squeeze** — agents doing fewer deals seek better economics
2. **Technology gap** — agents at legacy brokerages want modern tools
3. **Commission compression** — agents need higher splits to maintain income
4. **AI/tech hype** — agents hearing about "AI-powered brokerages" are curious
5. **Burnout** — low-support brokerages losing people who need hand-holding

### Spyglass Advantage in This Environment
- **85/15 split with $12K cap** (REAL Brokerage) = best economics in Austin
- **Mission Control** — legitimate tech differentiator, not vaporware
- **Pulse** (50+ data layers) — no other Austin brokerage has this
- **#1 Zillow conversion team** — proof of lead gen capability
- **3,400 sq ft office** — agents want community, not just a cloud model

### For Listing Agents
- **Pricing strategy is everything.** 53% price-cut rate means overpriced homes die.
- 69 DOM is the benchmark — if a listing doesn't get an offer in 3 weeks, reassess price.
- Emphasize staging, professional photography, and video tours.

- The "buy now, refi later" pitch is real if rates hit 5.5% by EOY.

### For All Agents
- Spring is make-or-break for annual goals. Q1 pipeline → Q2 closings.
- Use the Agent Production Planner to reverse-engineer activity numbers.
