# 📊 Austin Market Intelligence Brief — February 2026

## The Big Picture

The Austin market is in **correction-mode stabilization**. After the 2021-2022 frenzy and the 2023-2024 cooldown, we're entering a new phase: a balanced-to-buyer-favorable market that rewards **skilled agents** over lucky ones.

**This is actually great for Spyglass.** Here's why.

---

| Pending Listings | 3,630 | -- | Activity index: 22% |

### What the Numbers Mean

1. **Prices are stabilizing, not crashing.** The -3.3% YoY is moderating from deeper 2024 dips. Analysts expect 1-3% further softening through mid-2026, then stabilization.

2. **Inventory is healthy.** 4.2 months is textbook balanced market. Not the 1-month frenzy of 2021, not the 6+ month buyer's paradise of late 2024. Goldilocks zone.

3. **88 days on market = agents MUST deliver value.** The "stick a sign in the yard" era is over. Sellers need marketing, pricing strategy, staging advice. This is where Spyglass wins.

4. **Close-to-list at 90.6% means negotiation skills matter.** Agents who can negotiate get hired. Agents who can't lose clients.

## Strategic Implications for Spyglass

### 🎯 For Recruiting (179 → 300 Goal)

**The pitch writes itself in this market:**

> "In a balanced market, the brokerage you're with matters more than ever. When homes sold themselves, any brokerage worked. Now? You need real support — marketing, training, leads, technology. That's what Spyglass builds."

**Target agents at brokerages that can't compete on tech/support:**
- **KW agents** frustrated with outdated tech and franchise fees eating margins
- **eXp agents** who feel alone (cloud model = no physical support, no mentorship)
- **Compass agents** watching their stock crater and wondering about the merger
- **Independent agents** who can't afford their own marketing tech stack
- **New agents** (2022-2023 licensees) who've never seen a real market and need training

**Key stats to use in recruiting:**
- Austin has 12,851 active listings but only 2,514 closed sales/month = ~20% conversion rate
- 88 days average DOM means agents need to manage expectations AND market actively
- Agents with brokerage-generated leads close 3-5x more than cold prospectors

### 📈 For Agent Production (Double to $1B)

**Focus areas to help existing agents produce more:**

1. **Pricing Strategy Training** — In a -3.3% market, agents who overprice by 5% sit for months. Teach agents to use Pulse V2 data for hyper-local pricing.

2. **Buyer Opportunity Campaign** — 4.2 months inventory + lower prices = best buyer market since 2019. Agents should be reaching out to every renter and "someday" buyer in their sphere.

3. **Listing Marketing** — 88 DOM means agents need video, social, targeted digital ads. Spyglass Studio (when complete) will be a game-changer.

4. **SOI Reactivation** — Past clients who bought in 2021-2022 have equity concerns. Agents should proactively reach out with market updates (builds trust, generates referrals even if clients don't move).
