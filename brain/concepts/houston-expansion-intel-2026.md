## 7. Regulatory & Operational Differences (Austin → Houston)

| Factor | Impact |
|--------|--------|
| **TREC Licensing** | Same state license, no additional Houston requirements |
| **MLS** | Houston uses HAR MLS (separate from Austin Board). Must join HAR. ~$700/yr dues. |
| **HAR Membership** | HAR.com is THE Houston real estate platform. Much more dominant than ABoR's platform. Agents MUST be on HAR. |
| **Flood Disclosure** | Houston has MUCH stricter flood disclosure requirements. Agents need flood zone training. |
| **No Zoning** | Houston has no formal zoning (unique among major US cities). Different approach to property valuation and neighborhoods. |
| **Property Tax** | Harris County rates vary (2.0-2.5%) — higher than Travis County in many areas. |
| **HOA Prevalence** | Master-planned communities dominate Houston suburbs. HOA expertise critical. |
| **Hurricanes & Insurance** | Agents need to understand windstorm insurance, flood insurance. Major buyer concern. |
| **TXR Forms** | Same Texas REALTORS forms across state. No difference. |

**Key action:** Any Austin agent expanding to Houston needs HAR MLS access and flood zone training.
