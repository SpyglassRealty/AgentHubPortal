# Austin Brokerage Competitive Landscape — Q1 2026

## The Big Picture: Compass-Anywhere Mega-Merger

The single biggest event reshaping Austin's brokerage landscape is the **Compass-Anywhere merger**, which closed on **January 9, 2026** — months ahead of schedule. Key facts:

- **Combined value:** ~$10 billion
- **Combined agents:** ~340,000 globally
- **Brands under umbrella:** Compass, Century 21, Coldwell Banker, Sotheby's International Realty, Better Homes and Gardens Real Estate, Corcoran
- **Austin impact:** Compass already acquired Realty Austin in 2023, adding 600+ agents. Now they also bring in all Anywhere brands locally.
- **Industry share:** Top 3 brokerages (Compass, Anywhere, eXp) controlled 17% of U.S. sales volume in 2024. Top 10% of brokerages = 42% of volume.

### What This Means for Spyglass

**Opportunity:**
- Mergers create chaos. Agents hate change. When Compass absorbed Realty Austin, many agents felt lost in the corporate machine. The Anywhere integration will be even messier — different cultures, tech stacks, commission structures.
- **Disaffected agents are YOUR best recruiting pool.** Former Realty Austin agents who don't like Compass culture. Century 21 / Coldwell Banker agents suddenly under Compass umbrella who didn't sign up for that.
- Independent boutique positioning becomes MORE valuable as the megacorps consolidate. "You're a person, not a number" resonates harder when Compass has 340K agents.

**Threat:**
- Compass has massive tech investment (their platform, marketing tools, CRM). Hard to compete on tech budget alone.
- Their brand recognition and marketing spend dwarf independents.
- They can offer more resources per agent (staging, photography, etc.).

**Spyglass Counter-Strategy:**
1. **Tech that actually works** — Mission Control, Pulse, Beacon, Studio. Build tools BETTER than what Compass offers, because you're building for YOUR agents, not 340K strangers.
2. **Community & culture** — Boutique feel. Agents know Ryan. They're not ticket #47,832.
3. **Revenue share / splits** — Highlight that Spyglass keeps agents' money in their pockets.
4. **Recruiting messaging:** "Why be a number at Compass when you can be family at Spyglass?"

## Where Agents Are Moving (Recruiting Intelligence)

Based on Beacon data and industry trends:

**Outflows (agents leaving):**
- KW → Compass, eXp (agents wanting tech/brand upgrade)
- Century 21 / Coldwell Banker → Independent (merger uncertainty)
- RE/MAX → eXp, Real Broker (seeking better splits)
- eXp → Real Broker, independents (eXp culture fatigue)

**Key insight from Beacon:** Brokerage changes accelerate in Q1 (after year-end reviews, before spring market). February-March is PRIME recruiting season.

**Target profiles for Spyglass:**
1. **eXp agents doing 15-30 deals/year** who want local community and actual office support
2. **Former Realty Austin agents** uncomfortable in Compass corporate culture
3. **KW agents** tired of the profit share pyramid but not ready for virtual-only
4. **New agents** (1-3 years) who feel lost at big brokerages and need mentorship
5. **Houston agents** looking for a brokerage with actual Austin-Houston infrastructure
