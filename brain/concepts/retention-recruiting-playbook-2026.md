## Executive Summary

Getting from 179 → 300 agents requires both aggressive recruiting AND exceptional retention. Industry data shows the average brokerage loses 20-30% of agents annually. At 179 agents, even a 20% churn rate means losing ~36 agents/year — requiring you to recruit 157 agents just to hit 300. Cut churn to 10%, and you only need to recruit 139. **Every 1% improvement in retention saves ~2 agents and the $15-25K cost of replacing them.**

2026 is a strategic recruiting window: transaction volume is down, commission pressure is rising, split dissatisfaction is increasing, and technology gaps are becoming visible across competing brokerages. All four conditions that historically trigger agent migration are present simultaneously.

## Part 1: Retention Intelligence

### The Churn Problem
- **Industry average tenure:** 5 years at a brokerage (NAR 2025)
- **Most dangerous periods:** Month 10-14 (year-1 anniversary) and month 22-26 (year-2)
- **#1 reason agents leave:** "I don't feel supported" (not money — though money is the excuse)
- **#2 reason:** Peer/mentor departure creating a social vacuum
- **#3 reason:** Production dip with no intervention

### Early Warning Signals (What to Watch)
| Signal | Risk Level | Detection Method |
|--------|-----------|-----------------|
| Production drop >25% QoQ | 🔴 Critical | MLS data / GHL tracking |
| Missed 3+ team meetings | 🟠 High | Attendance records |
| LinkedIn headline change | 🟠 High | Manual monitoring |
| Reduced Slack/communication | 🟡 Moderate | Activity metrics |
| Approaching anniversary | 🟡 Moderate | Tenure tracking |
| Peer/mentor departure | 🟠 High | Exit tracking |
| Competitor contact/recruiting event | 🔴 Critical | Agent self-report / intel |

### Retention Playbooks

#### ☕ Coffee Check-In (For Early Warning Signals)
- **Trigger:** Engagement drop, 2+ weeks no communication
- **Timing:** Within 7 days of signal detection
- **Who runs it:** Ryan or team lead
- **Script:** "Hey [name], been meaning to catch up — want to grab coffee this week? Just want to hear how things are going."
- **Goal:** Listen 80%, talk 20%. Identify the real issue.
- **Follow-up:** Address concerns within 48 hours

#### 📈 Production Boost (For Volume Drops)
- **Trigger:** Volume down >25% YoY
- **Offer:** 2-3 warm brokerage leads, co-listing opportunity, targeted training
- **Timeline:** 30-day check-in after intervention
- **Escalation:** If no improvement after 60 days, direct 1:1 with Ryan

#### 🏆 Proactive Recognition (For Top Performers)
- **Trigger:** Top 20% producers, positive trend, major deal closed
- **Frequency:** Monthly Slack shoutout, quarterly award, annual event
- **Key insight:** Top producers leave when they feel taken for granted, not because of money

#### 💰 Split Review (For High-Volume Approaching Cap)
- **Trigger:** Agent approaching cap or doing >$200K GCI
- **CRITICAL:** Do this BEFORE they ask. Proactive >> reactive.
- **Prep:** Pull their numbers, compare to competitor offerings, prepare Spyglass value calc

### Onboarding Optimization (0-6 Month Retention)
The 0-6 month cohort has the highest churn (~25%). Every agent who leaves in their first year is a recruiting failure AND a retention failure.

**The 90-Day Success Framework:**
1. **Week 1:** Welcome kit, tech setup, meet their mentor, introduce to 5 team members
2. **Week 2:** First training session, CRM walkthrough, first lead assignment
3. **Week 4:** Check-in with Ryan (even 5 minutes = massive impact)
4. **Day 30:** Production check — did they get a lead? Start a transaction?
5. **Day 60:** Mid-point review with mentor
6. **Day 90:** Formal check-in — are they hitting milestones?

**Key metric:** Agents who get ≥3 brokerage leads in first 90 days have ~92% 1-year retention.

## Part 2: Recruiting Strategy

### 2026 Market Landscape — "The Great Reshuffle"
The market is repositioning, not crashing. Agents who relied on momentum volume are under pressure. When production slows, agents evaluate: split structure, lead support, technology, market intelligence tools, and brokerage leadership.

**Who's movable in 2026:**
- **Top performers** want: Stability, data visibility, expansion opportunity, better brand
- **Average performers** want: Support, leads, mentorship, structure
- **Both groups are movable** — for different reasons

### Competitor Weaknesses to Exploit

#### Keller Williams
- Aging tech platform (though KW Command improving)
- Market Center model creates inconsistent experience
- Many agents feel "nickled and dimed" with desk fees + tech fee + franchise fee
- **Spyglass angle:** "What if your brokerage tech actually worked and you didn't pay 5 different fees?"

#### Compass
- Still losing money at the corporate level
- Concierge program creates agent debt
- Premium brand but premium cost
- Austin presence shrinking since 2023 layoffs
- **Spyglass angle:** "Same quality brand, local ownership, no corporate debt on your back"

#### eXp Realty
- Virtual model = isolation for many agents
- Revenue share recruiting creates "MLM vibes"
- No local office presence
- Stock incentives worth less in current market
- **Spyglass angle:** "Real office, real people, real support — not a Zoom call"

#### REAL Brokerage
- Very aggressive in Austin, specifically targeting 1-2 year agents
- Similar virtual model to eXp
- Revenue share + stock incentives
- Low cap ($12K) attractive to high producers
- **Spyglass angle:** "They'll match our split. They can't match our leads, tools, or community"

#### Realty Austin
- Local competitor with strong brand recognition
- Traditional model, slow to adopt tech
- Good training but limited tech tools
- **Spyglass angle:** "Similar brand presence, dramatically better technology and support"

### The Spyglass Value Proposition (2026)

**For Recruiting Conversations — What Makes Us Different:**

1. **Real Technology, Not Vaporware**
   - Mission Control: Custom-built dashboards, market intelligence, CMA tools
   - Pulse: Real-time market data visualization with 50+ data layers
   - AI-powered tools that agents at other brokerages don't have access to

2. **Real Leads, Not Empty Promises**
   - Spyglass IDX site generating organic leads
   - MoveToAustin.org content driving relocation leads
   - Lead distribution based on merit and engagement

3. **Real Community, Not a Virtual Call**
   - 179 agents who actually know each other
   - Austin + Houston offices (physical presence)
   - Team culture that shows up at events, trainings, and for each other

4. **Real Economics**
   - Competitive splits designed for each production level
   - No hidden fees / no nickel-and-diming
   - Cap structure that rewards production

5. **Real Growth**
   - Scaling toward $1B — agents benefit from a growing brand
   - Expansion markets = new opportunities
   - AI-powered tools being built in-house (not bought from vendors)

### Outreach Cadence
1. **Day 1:** Initial outreach (text or email)
2. **Day 4:** Follow-up #1 (different channel)
3. **Day 10:** Follow-up #2 (value-add: share a market report or tool demo)
4. **Day 21:** Break-up message (creates urgency without pressure)
5. **Day 45+:** Nurture (monthly market updates, event invites)

**80% of recruiting happens after the 3rd touch.** Most recruiters give up after 1.

## Part 3: The Math — 179 → 300

### Scenario Modeling

| Metric | Current | Conservative | Aggressive |
|--------|---------|-------------|-----------|
| Starting agents | 179 | 179 | 179 |
| Annual churn rate | ~20% | 15% | 10% |
| Agents lost/year | 36 | 27 | 18 |
| Agents needed to hire | 157 | 148 | 139 |
| Monthly recruiting pace | 13/mo | 12.3/mo | 11.6/mo |
| Time to 300 | 12 mo | 12 mo | 12 mo |

**The retention lever is powerful:** Cutting churn from 20% to 10% saves 18 agents — that's 1.5 months of recruiting effort saved.

### Revenue Impact
At an average of $60K GCI per agent:
- 300 agents × $60K = **$18M gross** (vs. $10.7M at 179)
- Even at conservative 8% brokerage margin: **$1.44M net margin**
- Each retained agent = $60K revenue preserved = ~$4,800 margin

### Investment Priorities (Ranked by ROI)
1. **🥇 Onboarding system** — Fix the 0-6 month churn ($0 cost, immediate impact)
2. **🥈 Retention signals dashboard** — Catch flight risk early (built tonight — see tool)
3. **🥉 Boomerang campaign** — Highest win rate at 55% (cheapest agents to recruit)
4. **4️⃣ Beacon/pipeline optimization** — Scale recruiting outreach
5. **5️⃣ Split tier review** — Competitive analysis against eXp/REAL/Compass
